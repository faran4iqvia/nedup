## Introduction

At A2 you said **Ik vind dat**, **Ik denk dat**, and **Daar ben ik het mee eens**. B1 adds **nuance**: how strongly you state a view, how sure you are, and how polite you sound. **Naar mijn mening** is softer than a bare **Ik vind dat**; **Het lijkt me dat** hedges; **Ik ben er niet zeker van of** admits doubt; **Ik ben ervan overtuigd dat** strengthens. After this lesson you can discuss plans, news, and work strategy the way Dutch speakers do in meetings and media.

## Quick Review

From [A2 Lesson 5](/notes/a2/05-opinions): **vinden**, **denken**, **dat** + verb last, agreeing and disagreeing. From [B1 Lesson 5](/notes/b1/05-zou-politeness-and-advice): **zou** for soft requests and **Ik zou zeggen**; **Dat zou kunnen**. From [B1 Lesson 12](/notes/b1/12-word-order-masterclass): opinion chunks in main and subordinate clauses. Today you build a **toolbox** of nuanced opinion phrases.

## Grammar and Language Explanation

### A strength scale for opinions

| Strength | Dutch chunk | Feel |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Very soft | **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** … | seems to me |
| Soft | **Naar** **mijn** **mening** … | in my opinion |
| Hedged | **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** … | I would say |
| Neutral | **Ik** **denk** **dat** … | I think |
| Confident | **Ik** **ben** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** … | I am convinced |
| Strong | **Zonder** **twijfel** … | without doubt |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **wij** **meer** **tijd** **nodig** **hebben**. | It seems to me we need more time. |
| **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **is** **het** **plan** **haalbaar**. | In my opinion the plan is achievable. |
| **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** **wij** **moeten** **wachten**. | I would say we should wait. |
| **Ik** **ben** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** **dit** **werkt**. | I am convinced this works. |

### Softening: naar mijn mening, mijns inziens, wat mij betreft

| Chunk | Register | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **naar mijn mening** | neutral, common | **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **is** **het** **te** **laat**. |
| **mijns inziens** | formal, written | **Mijns** **inziens** **klopt** **het** **rapport**. |
| **wat mij betreft** | personal scope | **Wat** **mij** **betreft**, **kunnen** **wij** **stoppen**. |
| **persoonlijk gezien** | informal | **Persoonlijk** **gezien** **vind** ik **het** **goed**. |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **moeten** **wij** **eerst** **testen**. | In my opinion we must test first. |
| **Wat** **mij** **betreft**, **is** **de** **deadline** **realistisch**. | As far as I am concerned, the deadline is realistic. |
| **Persoonlijk** **gezien** **zou** ik **niet** **tekenen**. | Personally I would not sign. |

These chunks usually start the sentence or clause. The **verb** still follows normal **V2** or **dat** rules.

### Het lijkt me dat / het lijkt me + adjective

**Het lijkt me** + **dat** clause or + adjective:

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** … | **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **hij** **gelijk** **heeft**. |
| **Het** **lijkt** me\*\* + adj\*\* | **Het** **lijkt** me **een** **goed** **idee**. |
| **Het** **lijkt** me **onwaarschijnlijk** **dat** … | **Het** **lijkt** me **onwaarschijnlijk** **dat** **zij** **komt**. |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **wij** **meer** **data** **nodig** **hebben**. | It seems to me we need more data. |
| **Het** **lijkt** me **riskant** **om** **nu** **te** **lanceren**. | It seems risky to me to launch now. |
| **Het** **lijkt** me **niet** **verstandig** **dat** **wij** **haasten**. | It does not seem wise to me that we rush. |

From [B1 Lesson 5](/notes/b1/05-zou-politeness-and-advice): **Dat zou kunnen** as a short hedge.

### zou for hedged opinions

| Chunk | Example |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** … | **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** **het** **kan**. |
| **Het** **zou** **kunnen** **zijn** **dat** … | **Het** **zou** **kunnen** **zijn** **dat** **hij** **komt**. |
| **Ik** **zou** **niet** **durven** **zeggen** **dat** … | **Ik** **zou** **niet** **durven** **zeggen** **dat** **het** **klaar** **is**. |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** **wij** **nog** **één** **week** **nodig** **hebben**. | I would say we need one more week. |
| **Het** **zou** **kunnen** **zijn** **dat** **de** **deadline** **verschuift**. | It could be that the deadline shifts. |
| **Dat** **zou** **kunnen**, **maar** **ik** **ben** **niet** **zeker**. | That could be, but I am not sure. |

### Uncertainty: ik ben er niet zeker van of

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **ben** **er** **niet** **zeker** **van** **of** … | **Ik** **ben** **er** **niet** **zeker** **van** **of** **hij** **komt**. |
| **Ik** **weet** **niet** **of** … | **Ik** **weet** **niet** **of** **het** **lukt**. |
| **Voor** **zover** ik **weet** … | **Voor** **zover** ik **weet**, **is** **alles** **op** **schema**. |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **ben** **er** **niet** **zeker** **van** **of** **dit** **de** **juiste** **aanpak** **is**. | I am not sure whether this is the right approach. |
| **Voor** **zover** ik **kan** **beoordelen**, **is** **het** **plausibel**. | As far as I can judge, it is plausible. |
| **Naar** **wat** ik **begrijp**, **wordt** **de** **audit** **versneld**. | From what I understand, the audit is being speeded up. |

**of** + statement order inside the clause: **of hij komt**, not **of komt hij**.

### Strengthening: convinced and no doubt

| Chunk | Example |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **ben** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** … | **Ik** **ben** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** **het** **werkt**. |
| **Zonder** **twijfel** … | **Zonder** **twijfel** **de** **beste** **optie**. |
| **Ik** **sta** **er** **volledig** **achter**. | I fully stand behind it. |
| **Ik** **durf** **te** **stellen** **dat** … | I dare to state that … |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **ben** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** **wij** **op** **tijd** **zijn**. | I am convinced we are on time. |
| **Zonder** **twijfel** **is** **kwaliteit** **belangrijker** **dan** **snelheid**. | Without doubt quality is more important than speed. |
| **Ik** **sta** **achter** **het** **voorstel**. | I support the proposal. |

Use strong chunks sparingly in Dutch meetings. Overuse sounds pushy.

### Partial and mixed views

| Chunk | Use |
| --- | --- |
| **Tot** **op** **zekere** **hoogte** … | to a certain extent |
| **In** **zekere** **zin** … | in a certain sense |
| **Deels** … / **Gedeeltelijk** … | partly |
| **Ik** **heb** **gemengde** **gevoelens** **over** … | mixed feelings |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Tot** **op** **zekere** **hoogte** **ben** ik **het** **eens**. | To a certain extent I agree. |
| **In** **zekere** **zin** **heeft** hij **gelijk**. | In a certain sense he is right. |
| **Ik** **heb** **gemengde** **gevoelens** **over** **het** **plan**. | I have mixed feelings about the plan. |
| **Deels** **plausibel**, **deels** **onrealistisch**. | Partly plausible, partly unrealistic. |

From [B1 Lesson 1](/notes/b1/01-conjunctions-overview): combine with **hoewel**: **Hoewel ik sceptisch ben, lijkt me het plan deels haalbaar**.

### Sceptical and critical tone

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **ben** **sceptisch** **over** **de** **nieuwe** **aanpak**. | I am sceptical about the new approach. |
| **Dat** **klinkt** **twijfelachtig**. | That sounds doubtful. |
| **Ik** **neig** **naar** **het** **idee** **dat** **wij** **moeten** **wachten**. | I tend toward the idea that we should wait. |
| **Ik** **ben** **geneigd** **te** **denken** **dat** … | I am inclined to think that … |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Het** **vooroordeel** **tegen** **verandering** **is** **begrijpelijk**. | Prejudice against change is understandable. |
| **Objectief** **gezien** **is** **het** **moeilijk** **te** **beoordelen**. | Objectively speaking it is difficult to judge. |
| **Subjectief** **gezien** **vind** ik **het** **riskant**. | Subjectively I find it risky. |

### Formal written chunks

| Chunk | Context |
| --- | --- |
| **Mijns** **inziens** | reports, formal email |
| **Naar** **mijn** **bescheiden** **mening** | very polite hedge |
| **Voor** **zover** ik **weet** | cautious factual claim |
| **Vanuit** **mijn** **perspectief** | analysis, essays |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Mijns** **inziens** **vereist** **het** **plan** **aanpassing**. | In my view the plan requires adjustment. |
| **Naar** **mijn** **bescheiden** **mening** **zouden** **wij** **kunnen** **wachten**. | In my humble opinion we could wait. |
| **Vanuit** **mijn** **perspectief** **is** **de** **risico** **acceptabel**. | From my perspective the risk is acceptable. |

### Combining with B1 grammar

| Topic | Nuanced example |
| --- | --- |
| B1 L5 **zou** | **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** **wij** **moeten** **wachten**. |
| B1 L6 **als** unreal | **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **wij** **sneller** **zouden** **zijn** **als** **wij** **meer** **hadden** **voorbereid**. |
| B1 L10 indirect | **Hij** **zei** **dat** **het** **naar** **zijn** **mening** **te** **laat** **was**. |
| B1 L12 word order | **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **moet** **alles** **worden** **gecontroleerd** **voordat** **wij** **indienen**. |

### Preview: agreeing and debating (Lesson 14)

Nuanced opinions lead into **debate**: **Daar ben ik het deels mee eens**, **Ik zie dat anders**, **Laten we het oneens zijn**. Full agree/disagree tactics come in [Lesson 14](/notes/b1/14-agreeing-and-debating).

  In **Naar mijn mening is het haalbaar**, keep **naar mijn mening** as one rhythm group, then **is het**. Do not stress **mening** too heavily. See [Connected Speech](/notes/pronunciation/05-connected-speech).

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Double verb in dat after opinion chunk</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **is** **het** **goed**.

    ✅ **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **het** **goed** **is**.

    After **dat**, verb last: subject + rest + **verb**.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Using ik vind dat for everything</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik** **vind** **dat** **wij** **moeten** **wachten**. (in a delicate meeting)

    ✅ **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **wij** **moeten** **wachten**. / **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** …

    Dutch meetings often prefer softer chunks over blunt **Ik vind dat**.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Inversion after of in uncertainty</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik** **ben** **er** **niet** **zeker** **van** **of** **komt** **hij**.

    ✅ **Ik** **ben** **er** **niet** **zeker** **van** **of** **hij** **komt**.

    After **of**, use statement order.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Overusing ervan overtuigd</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik** **ben** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** **misschien** **het** **kan**. (**misschien** + **overtuigd** clash)

    ✅ **Ik** **denk** **dat** **het** **misschien** **kan**. / **Het** **zou** **kunnen**.

    Match strength words: **misschien** with hedges, **overtuigd** with confidence.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Strategiebespreking op kantoor</CardTitle>
    <CardDescription>Mila and Sam discuss whether to launch early, using hedged and partial opinions.</CardDescription>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Mila:** **Wat** **vind** jij **van** **het** **voorstel** **om** **vroeg** **te** **lanceren**?
    >
    > **Sam:** **Het** **lijkt** me **dat** **wij** **meer** **data** **nodig** **hebben**. **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **is** **het** **riskant**.
    >
    > **Mila:** **Ik** **ben** **er** **niet** **zeker** **van** **of** **de** **kwaliteit** **al** **goed** **genoeg** **is**.
    >
    > **Sam:** **Voor** **zover** ik **kan** **beoordelen**, **is** **het** **deels** **plausibel**. **Tot** **op** **zekere** **hoogte** **snap** ik **de** **haast**.
    >
    > **Mila:** **Ik** **heb** **gemengde** **gevoelens** **over** **de** **aanpak**. **Persoonlijk** **gezien** **zou** ik **wachten**.
    >
    > **Sam:** **Ik** **zou** **zeggen** **dat** **wij** **nog** **één** **sprint** **nodig** **hebben**. **Ik** **ben** **niet** **ervan** **overtuigd** **dat** **nu** **het** **juiste** **moment** **is**.
    >
    > **Mila:** **Dat** **klinkt** **geloofwaardig**. **Ik** **ben** **sceptisch** **over** **versnelling** **zonder** **test**.
    >
    > **Sam:** **Mijns** **inziens** **moeten** **wij** **objectief** **beoordelen** **voordat** **wij** **besluiten**. **Wat** **mij** **betreft**, **pleiten** **wij** **voor** **kwaliteit** **boven** **snelheid**.

    **In English:**

    > **Mila:** What do you think of the proposal to launch early?
    >
    > **Sam:** It seems to me we need more data. In my opinion it is risky.
    >
    > **Mila:** I am not sure whether the quality is already good enough.
    >
    > **Sam:** As far as I can judge, it is partly plausible. To a certain extent I understand the rush.
    >
    > **Mila:** I have mixed feelings about the approach. Personally I would wait.
    >
    > **Sam:** I would say we need one more sprint. I am not convinced that now is the right moment.
    >
    > **Mila:** That sounds credible. I am sceptical about speeding up without testing.
    >
    > **Sam:** In my view we must assess objectively before we decide. As far as I am concerned, we advocate quality over speed.
