## Introduction

You have completed all twenty eight B1 lessons, from extended conjunctions through practical Netherlands to the integration capstone. This chapter brings everything together: a topic by topic summary, a combined vocabulary reference, a mixed test, and a clear picture of what you can do at B1 level. Take your time, say answers aloud, and revisit any lesson where you hesitated. Passing most of this test means you have reached solid B1 independence in Dutch.

### Future, zou, and hypotheticals

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Lessons 4 to 7</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **Zullen** for future and suggestions: **Ik zal je morgen bellen**, **Zullen we afspreken?** **Zou** for politeness and soft advice: **Zou je me kunnen helpen?**, **Ik zou wachten**. Hypotheticals with **als**: **Als ik rijk was, zou ik reizen**. Conditional past: **Als ik had geweten, was ik gekomen** / **zou ik gekomen zijn**. **Had** + participle in the **als** clause, **zou** + infinitive or **was** + participle in the main clause.

    **Key patterns:** Zou u dat kunnen uitleggen? · Als het morgen regent, blijf ik thuis. · Als ik tijd had gehad, had ik je gebeld.

    See [Lesson 4](/notes/b1/04-future-with-zullen), [Lesson 5](/notes/b1/05-zou-politeness-and-advice), [Lesson 6](/notes/b1/06-hypotheticals-with-als), [Lesson 7](/notes/b1/07-conditional-past).

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Lessons 11 and 12</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
