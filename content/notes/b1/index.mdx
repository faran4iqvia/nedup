## Welcome to B1 🚀

This is the level of independence: reading the news, writing emails, handling
meetings, and following natural, fast Dutch. You'll master complex word order,
hypotheticals, the passive voice, and the nuances that make Dutch sound Dutch.

**What you'll learn:**

- Conjunctions, relative clauses, and _waar_ + preposition
- The future with _zullen_, politeness with _zou_, and hypotheticals with _als_
- The passive voice with _worden_ and _zijn_, and indirect speech
- Nuanced opinions, debating, formal letters, and work situations
- Diminutives, particles, idioms, and fast-speech strategies
- Practical Netherlands: gemeente, BSN, zorgverzekering, and daily bureaucracy

Finish with the [B1 Review and Test](/notes/b1/review-and-test) to check you
have reached solid B1 independence.
