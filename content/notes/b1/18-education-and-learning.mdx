## Introduction

You already **studeren** and talk about **de les** and **de universiteit** from A1. B1 adds the full **Dutch education landscape**: **vwo**, **havo**, **mbo**, **hogeschool**, **tentamen**, **scriptie**, and **inschrijving**. You also learn how Dutch speakers talk about **lifelong learning** with **bijscholing** and **levenslang leren**. After this lesson you can discuss your study path, exams, and learning goals the way people do in the Netherlands and Flanders.

## Grammar and Language Explanation

### The Dutch education ladder

Dutch education has clear labels. At B1 you recognise them in conversation and forms:

| Stage | Dutch | Age (rough) | Leads to |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Primary | **de** **basisschool** | 4 to 12 | secondary track |
| Secondary | **de** **middelbare** **school** | 12 to 16/18 | further study |
| Academic prep | **het** **vwo** | | university |
| General sec. | **het** **havo** | | hogeschool / some uni |
| Vocational | **het** **mbo** | | work or further study |
| Applied uni | **de** **hogeschool** | | bachelor (applied) |
| Research uni | **de** **universiteit** | | bachelor, master, PhD |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Na** **de** **basisschool** **gaat** **zij** **naar** **het** **vwo**. | After primary school she goes to pre university track. |
| **Hij** **volgt** **een** **mbo-opleiding** **als** **verpleger**. | He follows an MBO programme as a nurse. |
| **Ik** **studeer** **aan** **de** **hogeschool** **in** **Utrecht**. | I study at the university of applied sciences in Utrecht. |

**Studeren aan** + institution: **Ik studeer aan de universiteit**. Informal: **Ik zit op de middelbare school**.

### Opleiding, studie, and cursus

| Word | Use | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **de** **opleiding** | programme / training | **Mijn** **opleiding** **duurt** **vier** **jaar**. |
| **de** **studie** | study / degree course | **Ik** **doe** **een** **studie** **Communicatie**. |
| **de** **cursus** | course (shorter) | **Ik** **volg** **een** **cursus** **Nederlands**. |
| **de** **bacheloropleiding** | bachelor's programme | **De** **bacheloropleiding** **start** **in** **september**. |
| **de** **masteropleiding** | master's programme | **Na** **mijn** **bachelor** **wil** ik **een** **masteropleiding**. |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Wat** **is** **je** **studierichting**? | What is your field of study? |
| **Het** **studieprogramma** **staat** **online**. | The study programme is online. |
| **De** **studiekeuze** **is** **moeilijk**. | Choosing a study is difficult. |

### Exams, grades, slagen and zakken

From [A2 Lesson 13](/notes/a2/13-om-te): **slagen voor het examen**. B1 expands:

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **het** **examen** | exam (general) |
| **het** **tentamen** | university exam |
| **de** **toets** | test / quiz |
| **het** **cijfer** | grade / mark |
| **slagen** **voor** | to pass |
| **zakken** **voor** | to fail |
| **geslaagd** | passed |
| **gezakt** | failed |
| **de** **tentamenweek** | exam week |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ben** **je** **geslaagd**? | Did you pass? |
| **Helaas** **ben** ik **gezakt** **voor** **wiskunde**. | Unfortunately I failed maths. |
| **Ik** **heb** **een** **acht** **voor** **Nederlands**. | I got an eight for Dutch. |
| **Het** **cijfer** **staat** **online**. | The grade is online. |

Dutch grades run **1** (fail) to **10** (excellent). **6** is pass.

</Callout>

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">studeren op instead of aan</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik** **studeer** **op** **de** **universiteit**.

    ✅ **Ik** **studeer** **aan** **de** **universiteit**.

    Use **aan** for the institution. **Op** appears in **zit op school** (attend school), not **studeer op de universiteit**.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Confusing havo, vwo, and mbo</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ Treating **mbo** as university.

    ✅ **Mbo** = vocational. **Hogeschool** and **universiteit** = higher education after secondary.

    Know the ladder before you describe your path in interviews or small talk.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Grade scale shock</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Een** **zes** **is** **slecht**. (English 6 out of 10 feel)

    ✅ **Een** **zes** **is** **voldoende**. **Tien** **is** **uitmuntend**.

    Dutch marks: **6** = pass, **8** = very good, **10** = rare top score.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">volgen vs studeren</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik** **studeer** **een** **cursus** **van** **twee** **weken**.

    ✅ **Ik** **volg** **een** **cursus** **van** **twee** **weken**. / **Ik** **studeer** **aan** **de** **universiteit**.

    **Volgen** for a specific **cursus** or **vak**. **Studeren** for being a student at an institution.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

    > **Mila:** Where did you study?

    >
    > **Sam:** Nice. How is your study progress?

    >
    > **Sam:** Lifelong learning, right? Good luck!
