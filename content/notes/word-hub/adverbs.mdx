## About Dutch adverbs

**~35 adverbs** · Adverbs add _when_, _how often_, _where_, and _how much_. In Dutch they often start the sentence — and when they do, the verb comes second and the subject moves after it: **Vandaag werk ik thuis** ([Word Order](/notes/grammar/07-word-order-svo)).
