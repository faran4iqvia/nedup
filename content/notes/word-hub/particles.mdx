## About Dutch particles

**~22 particles** · Particles are small words that do not translate cleanly. They soften, emphasise, or make speech sound natural. You meet them everywhere in spoken Dutch — especially in [A2 Culture](/notes/a2/18-culture-traditions) and [Scenarios](/notes/scenarios/00-introduction).

<Callout title="Learn them in context" type="info">
  Do not memorise particles in isolation. Read the example column out loud — the meaning is in the **tone** and **situation**, not a one-word English gloss.
</Callout>

## Common particles

| Dutch | English feel | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| hoor | (softener / reassurance) | **Dat klopt**, hoor. · That's right, you know. |
| toch | yet / still / surely | **Kom** toch **mee!** · Do come along! |
| even | briefly / just | **Ik bel** even **aan.** · I'll just give a quick call. |
| nou | well / come on | **Nou**, wat vind je? · Well, what do you think? |
| zeg | (emphasis) | **Dat is** mooi, zeg. · That's beautiful, I must say. |
| eigenlijk | actually | **Ik ben** eigenlijk **moe.** · I'm actually tired. |
| gewoon | just / simply | **Doe** gewoon **normaal.** · Just act normal. |
| maar | just / go ahead | **Ga** maar **zitten.** · Go ahead and sit down. |
| eens | (try it / once) | **Kijk** eens **hier.** · Have a look here. |
| hè | right? / isn't it? | **Lekker**, hè? · Nice, isn't it? |

## Agreement and reaction

| Dutch | English feel | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ja hoor | yes, sure | **Ja** hoor, **geen probleem.** |
| nee hoor | no, not really | **Nee** hoor, **dat hoeft niet.** |
| zeker | certainly / definitely | **Dat is** zeker **waar.** |
| precies | exactly | **Precies** wat ik bedoelde. |
| inderdaad | indeed | **Inderdaad**, goed punt. |
| natuurlijk | of course | **Natuurlijk** kom ik. |

## Softening and politeness

| Dutch | English feel | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| misschien | maybe | **Misschien** morgen? |
| waarschijnlijk | probably | **Hij komt** waarschijnlijk **later.** |
| helaas | unfortunately | **Helaas**, vol geboekt. |
| gelukkig | fortunately | **Gelukkig** ben je er. |
| vast | surely / probably | **Dat komt** vast **goed.** |

Many of these also appear in [Adverbs](/notes/word-hub/adverbs#degree-and-manner) — particles lean more **spoken** and **emotional**.

## Word order note

Particles usually stay **close to the verb** or at the **end** of a phrase: **Kom even langs**, **Dat is gezellig**, hoor.

See [Grammar: Word Order](/notes/grammar/07-word-order-svo) and [A2 Culture](/notes/a2/18-culture-traditions).
