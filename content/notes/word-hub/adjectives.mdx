## About Dutch adjectives

An adjective **before** a noun usually takes **-e** (**de grote stad**, **een groot huis** is the exception: **het**-words with **een** stay uninflected). After the verb it stays plain: **de stad is groot**. See [Grammar Track: Adjectives](/notes/grammar/13-adjectives) for the full rule.
