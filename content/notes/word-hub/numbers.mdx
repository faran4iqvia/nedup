## About Dutch numbers

**~45 number forms** · Two things surprise English speakers: the **units come before the tens** (**eenentwintig** = one-and-twenty = 21), and Dutch uses a **comma** for decimals and a **point** for thousands (**€ 1.250,50**). See [Numbers and Money](/notes/a0/07-numbers-21-100-and-money).

## Ordinal numbers

Add **-de** or **-ste**. Use **-de** from 2 to 19 (except **eerste**, **derde**, **achtste**), and **-ste** from 20 upward. Dates use ordinals: **de eerste mei**.
