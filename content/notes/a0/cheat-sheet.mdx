## Greetings by time of day

| Time | Dutch | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Morning | **Goedemorgen** | Good morning |
| Afternoon | **Goedemiddag** | Good afternoon |
| Evening | **Goedenavond** | Good evening |
| Night (bedtime) | **Welterusten** | Good night (sleep) |
| Any time | **Hallo** / **Hoi** | Hi |
| Leaving | **Tot ziens** / **Dag** | Goodbye |
| Meeting someone | **Aangenaam** | Nice to meet you |

❌ **Welterusten** at 20:00 when you arrive · ✅ **Goedenavond**

## Essential phrases

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Hoe heet je?** | What is your name? |
| **Ik ben …** | I am … |
| **Hoe gaat het?** | How are you? |
| **Het gaat goed.** | I am fine. |
| **Dank je wel.** | Thank you. |
| **Alsjeblieft.** | Please / here you are |
| **Sorry** / **Pardon** | Sorry / excuse me |
| **Ik begrijp het niet.** | I do not understand. |
| **Langzaam, alsjeblieft.** | Slowly, please. |

## Question words

| Dutch | English | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **wie** | who | **Wie is dat?** |
| **wat** | what | **Wat is dat?** |
| **waar** | where | **Waar woon je?** |
| **wanneer** | when | **Wanneer?** |
| **hoe** | how | **Hoe heet je?** |
| **hoeveel** | how much/many | **Hoeveel kost het?** |
| **waarom** | why | **Waarom?** |

## Numbers 0–20

| | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 **nul** | 5 **vijf** | 10 **tien** | 15 **vijftien** |
| 1 **één** | 6 **zes** | 11 **elf** | 16 **zestien** |
| 2 **twee** | 7 **zeven** | 12 **twaalf** | 17 **zeventien** |
| 3 **drie** | 8 **acht** | 13 **dertien** | 18 **achttien** |
| 4 **vier** | 9 **negen** | 14 **veertien** | 19 **negentien** |

**20 = twintig** · **Hoe oud ben je?** → **Ik ben twintig jaar.**

## Numbers 21–100 (pattern)

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **eenentwintig** | 21 (one-and-twenty) |
| **tweeëndertig** | 32 |
| **vijftig** | 50 |
| **honderd** | 100 |

**Money:** **€5,50** = **vijf euro vijftig** · **Het kost tien euro.**

## de / het (primer summary)

| Article | When | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **de** | most nouns | **de man**, **de koffie**, **de tafel** |
| **het** | many neuter nouns | **het brood**, **het water**, **het huis** |

Learn **het brood**, not just **brood**. Full rules: [De/Het Primer](/notes/a0/12-de-het-primer).
