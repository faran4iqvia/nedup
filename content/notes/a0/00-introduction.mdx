## Welcome to A0 👋

This is the very beginning of your Dutch journey. No prior knowledge needed —
by the end of this level you can greet people, introduce yourself, count,
handle money, tell the time, and order food and drinks.

**What you'll learn:**

- Greetings, introductions, and the Dutch alphabet
- Numbers 0–100, age, money, days, months, and dates
- The essential verbs _zijn_ (to be) and _hebben_ (to have)
- Asking basic questions and saying where you live
- Ordering food and drink, and reading the clock
- **De** and **het**: learn every noun with its article ([Primer](/notes/a0/12-de-het-primer))

Finish with the [de/het Primer](/notes/a0/12-de-het-primer), then the [A0 Review and Test](/notes/a0/review-and-test). Keep [A0 Cheat Sheet](/notes/a0/cheat-sheet) open while you study. Look up words anytime in the [Word Hub](/notes/word-hub/00-introduction) — especially [Greetings & Phrases](/notes/word-hub/greetings-and-phrases). After A0, open [Vowel Sounds](/notes/pronunciation/01-vowel-sounds) and [Consonant Sounds](/notes/pronunciation/02-consonant-sounds) so your first Dutch is easy to understand.
