## Welcome to A1 🧱

You know the basics — now it's time to live daily life in Dutch. This level
covers the vocabulary and grammar you need for routines, family, shopping,
eating out, and your first past-tense stories.

**What you'll learn:**

- Daily routines, family, food, shopping, housing, hobbies, and weather
- Dutch word order (SVO and inversion) and negation with _niet_ / _geen_
- Possessives, demonstratives, plurals, and adjective endings
- The simple past of regular and irregular verbs
- Reflexive and separable verbs

Keep the [A1 Cheat Sheet](/notes/a1/cheat-sheet) open for word order, **niet** vs **geen**, and the top 50 verbs.
