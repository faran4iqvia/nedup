## Introduction

You can already say **Ik woon in Amsterdam** and **het kleine appartement**. Now you go inside: **de woonkamer**, **de slaapkamer**, **de keuken**, and **waar is de badkamer?** After this lesson you can describe your home room by room, talk about rent and neighbours, and say where furniture is with **liggen**, **staan**, and **zitten**, combined with adjectives from [Lesson 11](/notes/a1/11-adjectives) and plurals from [Lesson 10](/notes/a1/10-plural-nouns).

## Quick Review

From [A0 Lesson 9](/notes/a0/09-where-do-you-live): **wonen**, **het huis**, **het appartement**, **de flat**, and **Waar woon je?** From [Lesson 10](/notes/a1/10-plural-nouns): **de stoel**, **de kamer**, **de deur**, **het raam**, **de vloer**, **de kast**, **de trap**, **de tuin**. ## Grammar and Language Explanation

### Inside a Dutch home: main rooms

Dutch homes often have these core rooms. Learn each word **with de** or **het**:

| Dutch | English | Article |
| --- | --- | --- |
| de woonkamer | living room | de |
| de slaapkamer | bedroom | de |
| de keuken | kitchen | de |
| de badkamer | bathroom | de |
| de gang | hallway | de |
| het toilet | toilet / WC | het |
| het balkon | balcony | het |
| de zolder | attic | de |
| de kelder | basement / cellar | de |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| De **woonkamer** is groot. | The living room is big. |
| De **slaapkamer** is klein. | The bedroom is small. |
| Het **toilet** is op de gang. | The toilet is in the hallway. |
| Het **balkon** is op de tweede verdieping. | The balcony is on the second floor. |

**Kamer** from [Lesson 10](/notes/a1/10-plural-nouns) means any room. **Woonkamer** and **slaapkamer** are specific rooms inside the home.

### wonen (to live) — full review

This is the central verb of the lesson. You met **wonen** in A0. Here is the full table:

| Pronoun | wonen | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ik | woon | I live |
| jij / je | woont | you live |
| u | woont | you live (formal) |
| hij / zij / het | woont | he / she lives |
| wij / we | wonen | we live |
| jullie | wonen | you live (plural) |
| zij / ze | wonen | they live |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| Ik **woon** in een **groot** huis. | I live in a big house. |
| **Woon** je in een flat? | Do you live in a flat? |
| Zij **woont** op de **begane grond**. | She lives on the ground floor. |
| Wij **wonen** naast de buren. | We live next to the neighbours. |
| Ik **woon** **niet** in de stad. | I do not live in the city. |

### Floors and position: boven, beneden, verdieping

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| de verdieping | floor (level) |
| de begane grond | ground floor |
| boven | upstairs / above |
| beneden | downstairs / below |
| op de eerste verdieping | on the first floor (Dutch: one up from ground) |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| Ik woon op de **tweede verdieping**. | I live on the second floor. |
| De slaapkamer is **boven**. | The bedroom is upstairs. |
| De woonkamer is **beneden**. | The living room is downstairs. |
| De **buren** wonen **boven** ons. | The neighbours live above us. |

Note: Dutch floor counting often starts with **begane grond**, then **eerste verdieping**. This can differ from English. When in doubt, say **boven** or **beneden**.

### Where things are: liggen, staan, zitten

Dutch uses three verbs to say where something **is** in a physical sense. English often uses only "is":

| Verb | Used for | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **staan** | tall objects, people standing | De bank **staat** in de woonkamer. |
| **liggen** | flat objects lying down | Het boek **ligt** op de tafel. |
| **zitten** | things inside something, people sitting | De kat **zit** op de bank. |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| De stoelen **staan** in de keuken. | The chairs are in the kitchen. |
| Mijn bril **ligt** op de tafel. | My glasses are on the table. |
| Ik **zit** in de woonkamer. | I am sitting in the living room. |
| Waar **ligt** de sleutel? | Where is the key? (lying) |
| Waar **staat** de wasmachine? | Where is the washing machine? |

At A1, **Waar is …?** works for everything. **Liggen**, **staan**, and **zitten** make you sound more natural.

#### liggen, staan, zitten — Tier B grouped table

| Pronoun | liggen | staan | zitten |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ik | lig | sta | zit |
| jij / je | ligt | staat | zit |
| u | ligt | staat | zit |
| hij / zij / het | ligt | staat | zit |
| wij / jullie / zij | liggen | staan | zitten |

| Verb | Example 1 | Example 2 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **liggen** | Het boek **ligt** op tafel. <br /> _The book is on the table._ | **Liggen** de sleutels op de bank? <br /> _Are the keys on the sofa?_ |
| **staan** | De lamp **staat** op de kast. <br /> _The lamp is on the cupboard._ | **Waar staan** de stoelen? <br /> _Where are the chairs?_ |
| **zitten** | Wij **zitten** in de woonkamer. <br /> _We are sitting in the living room._ | De kat **zit** op het bed. <br /> _The cat is on the bed._ |

**Staan** also appears in **opstaan** from [Lesson 1](/notes/a1/01-daily-routines): **Ik sta om zeven uur op**. Here **staan** means to be positioned upright.

### Prepositions with rooms and furniture

| Preposition | Meaning | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **in** | in / inside | in de keuken |
| **op** | on | op de bank |
| **naast** | next to | naast het bed |
| **voor** | in front of | voor het raam |
| **achter** | behind | achter de deur |
| **tussen** | between | tussen de stoelen |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| De spiegel **staat voor** het bed. | The mirror is in front of the bed. |
| De tafel staat **tussen** de stoelen. | The table is between the chairs. |
| Het tapijt ligt **op** de vloer. | The rug is on the floor. |
| De buren wonen **naast** ons. | The neighbours live next to us. |

### Adjectives + housing

From [Lesson 11](/notes/a1/11-adjectives):

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| de **grote** woonkamer | the big living room |
| een **klein** appartement | a small apartment |
| de **schone** badkamer | the clean bathroom |
| de **nieuwe** wasmachine | the new washing machine |
| **deze grote** slaapkamer | this big bedroom |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| Ik woon in een **klein** maar **gezellig** huis. | I live in a small but cosy house. |
| **De open** deur is naar de tuin. | The open door is to the garden. |
| **Geen vieze** schoenen in huis! | No dirty shoes in the house! |

<Callout title="Pronunciation Focus" type="info">
  **Woonkamer** has that Dutch **oo** sound. **Keuken** and **meubilair** stress the first syllable. Say **WOON-ka-mer**, **KEU-ken**, **meu-bi-LAIR**. See [Vowel Sounds](/notes/pronunciation/01-vowel-sounds).
</Callout>

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Using zijn for where furniture is</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ De lamp **is** op de tafel. (understandable, but stiff)

    ✅ De lamp **staat** op de tafel.

    Dutch prefers **staan** / **liggen** / **zitten** for physical position. **Is** works in questions (**Waar is de keuken?**) but **staat** is better for objects.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Wrong article with rooms</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **het** keuken / **de** toilet

    ✅ **de** keuken / **het** toilet

    Learn **de keuken**, **het toilet**, **het bed** as chunks.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Confusing bank (sofa) with bank (financial)</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ Ik zit op de bank. (when meaning financial institution)

    ✅ Ik zit op **de bank**. = I am sitting on the **sofa**.

    Context makes it clear at home. **De bank** in a living room = sofa. **De bank** in the city centre = bank.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">Forgetting adjective e with de rooms</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ een **groot** woonkamer

    ✅ een **grote** woonkamer

    **Woonkamer** is a **de** word. Before the noun: **de grote woonkamer**, **een grote woonkamer**.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
