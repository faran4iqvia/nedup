## Introduction

Almost every **A0** purchase ends the same way: the total, **pin of contant?**, pay, **dank u wel**. This scenario works after the [café](/notes/scenarios/a0-cafe-ordering), [bakery](/notes/scenarios/a0-bakery-counter), or [market](/notes/scenarios/a0-asking-the-price). From [Numbers and Money](/notes/a0/07-numbers-21-100-and-money).

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Dat is € …** | That is € … |
| **Pin of contant?** | Card or cash? |
| **Pin, alstublieft.** | By card, please. |
| **Contant, alstublieft.** | Cash, please. |
| **Alstublieft.** | Here you are. (handing payment) |
| **Wilt u het bonnetje?** | Would you like the receipt? |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| betalen | to pay |
| pinnen | pay by debit card |
| contant | cash |
| de pinpas | debit card |
| het geld | money |
| wisselgeld | change |
| het bonnetje | receipt |
| samen | together (total) |
| te veel / te weinig | too much / too little |

## Key phrases

| # | Cashier | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Dat is €6,75.** | That is €6.75. | **Oké.** | OK. |
| 2 | **Pin of contant?** | Card or cash? | **Pin, alstublieft.** | By card, please. |
| 3 | **Hier is uw bonnetje.** | Here is your receipt. | **Dank u wel.** | Thank you. |
| 4 | **Wilt u het bonnetje?** | Would you like the receipt? | **Nee, dank u.** | No, thank you. |
| 5 | **U betaalt te veel.** | You are paying too much. | **Oh, sorry!** | Oh, sorry! |
| 6 | **Alstublieft, uw wisselgeld.** | Here is your change. | **Dank u wel.** | Thank you. |
| 7 | **Vriendelijke dag!** | Have a nice day! | **Tot ziens!** | Goodbye! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Aan de kassa</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Kassière:** Goedemiddag. Dat is samen €9,20.
    >
    > **Klant:** Alstublieft. Pin?
    >
    > **Kassière:** Ja hoor. _(klant pint)_ Wilt u het bonnetje?
    >
    > **Klant:** Nee, dank u. Fijne dag!
    >
    > **Kassière:** Dank u wel, tot ziens!

    **In English:**

    > **Cashier:** Good afternoon. That is €9.20 together.
    >
    > **Customer:** Here you are. Card?
    >
    > **Cashier:** Sure. _(customer taps card)_ Would you like the receipt?
    >
    > **Customer:** No, thank you. Have a nice day!
    >
    > **Cashier:** Thank you, goodbye!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Met contant</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Verkoper:** Dat is €3,50.
    >
    > **Klant:** Contant. _(geeft €5)_ Alstublieft.
    >
    > **Verkoper:** Dat is €1,50 wisselgeld. Dank u wel!

    **In English:**

    > **Seller:** That is €3.50.
    >
    > **Customer:** Cash. _(gives €5)_ Here you are.
    >
    > **Seller:** That is €1.50 change. Thank you!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
