## Introduction

You need **een taxi** from the station or after a night out. You say where to go, confirm the route, and pay. Uses [A1 Transport](/notes/a1/15-transport) and [Describing Places](/notes/a2/09-describing-places).

## Quick Review

| From your course | You use it here |
| --- | --- |
| [A1 L15](/notes/a1/15-transport): travel | **Naar het station, alstublieft.** |
| [A2 L9](/notes/a2/09-describing-places): location | **Het adres is Kerkstraat 14.** |
| [A0 Paying](/notes/scenarios/a0-paying-pin-or-cash): payment | **Kan ik pinnen?** |

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Naar …, alstublieft.** | To …, please. |
| **Kunt u mij naar … brengen?** | Can you take me to …? |
| **Hoeveel kost de rit ongeveer?** | How much does the ride cost approximately? |
| **Kunt u de meter gebruiken?** | Can you use the meter? |
| **Kunt u hier stoppen?** | Can you stop here? |

## Key phrases

| # | Driver | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Waar wilt u heen?** | Where would you like to go? | **Naar Schiphol, vertrekhal, alstublieft.** | To Schiphol, departures hall, please. |
| 2 | **Kent u het adres?** | Do you know the address? | **Ja: Stationsweg 8, Utrecht.** | Yes: Stationsweg 8, Utrecht. |
| 3 | **Ongeveer twintig euro.** | About twenty euros. | **Oké.** | OK. |
| 4 | **Wilt u pinnen of contant?** | Would you like to pay by card or cash? | **Pin, alstublieft.** | Card, please. |
| 5 | **We zijn er.** | We are here. | **Dank u wel. Houd het zo.** | Thank you. Keep the change. (optional) |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Van station naar huis</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Chauffeur:** Goedenavond. Waar naartoe?
    >
    > **Passagier:** Goedenavond. Naar de Neude 22 in het centrum, alstublieft.
    >
    > **Chauffeur:** Prima. Rond de vijftien euro, afhankelijk van het verkeer.
    >
    > **Passagier:** Geen probleem. Kunt u via de snelweg rijden?
    >
    > **Chauffeur:** Ja hoor. … We zijn er. Dat is €14,80.
    >
    > **Passagier:** Alstublieft. Dank u wel!

    **In English:**

    > **Driver:** Good evening. Where to?
    >
    > **Passenger:** Good evening. To Neude 22 in the city centre, please.
    >
    > **Driver:** Fine. Around fifteen euros, depending on traffic.
    >
    > **Passenger:** No problem. Can you take the motorway?
    >
    > **Driver:** Sure. … We are here. That is €14.80.
    >
    > **Passenger:** Here you are. Thank you!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
