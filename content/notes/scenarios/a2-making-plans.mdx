## Introduction

**Zullen we naar de film gaan?** At **A2** you make plans: suggest, accept, or decline with reasons using **omdat** or **want**. From [Making Plans](/notes/a1/24-making-plans-and-suggestions) and [Prepositions of Time](/notes/a2/11-prepositions-time).

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Zullen we …?** | Shall we …? |
| **Heb je zin om …?** | Do you feel like …? |
| **Wanneer past het jou?** | When suits you? |
| **Dat lijkt me leuk.** | That sounds nice to me. |
| **Ik kan niet, omdat …** | I cannot, because … |

## Key phrases

| # | Dutch | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Zullen we vanavond iets drinken?** | Shall we have a drink tonight? |
| 2 | **Waar en hoe laat?** | Where and what time? |
| 3 | **In de stad, rond acht uur?** | In town, around eight o'clock? |
| 4 | **Ik kan pas om half negen, omdat ik moet werken tot acht uur.** | I can only make half past eight, because I have to work until eight. |
| 5 | **Geen probleem.** | No problem. |
| 6 | **Half negen bij De Kroeg?** | Half past eight at De Kroeg? |
| 7 | **Tot dan!** | See you then! |
| 8 | **Tot vanavond!** | See you tonight! |

## Answer Key

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardContent>
    1. **Zullen we zaterdag naar de bioscoop gaan?** — Shall we go to the cinema on Saturday?
    2. **Ik kan niet mee, omdat ik ziek ben.** / **Helaas, ik ben ziek.** — I cannot come along, because I am ill. / Unfortunately, I am ill.
    3. **Omdat ik moe ben, ga ik niet mee.** — Because I am tired, I am not coming along.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
