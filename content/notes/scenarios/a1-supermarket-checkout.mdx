## Introduction

Dutch supermarkets (**de supermarkt**) are fast and self service heavy. At the **kassa** (checkout) you hear the same phrases every time. This scenario prepares you for Albert Heijn, Jumbo, Lidl, and similar stores.

Assumes **A1** from [Shopping](/notes/a1/08-shopping) and [Numbers & Money from A0](/notes/a0/07-numbers-21-100-and-money).

## Grammar and Language Explanation

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik wil graag** … | I would like … |
| **Heeft u** …? (cashier formal) | Do you have …? |
| **Dat is** + price | That is € … |
| **Alstublieft** / **Dank u wel** | Here you are. / Thank you. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| de supermarkt | supermarket |
| de kassa | checkout |
| de bonuskaart | loyalty card |
| de tas / het tasje | bag |
| pinnen | pay by card |
| contant | cash |
| het bonnetje | receipt |
| statiegeld | bottle deposit |
| zelfscankassa | self checkout |

## Key phrases

| # | Cashier | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Goedendag.** | Good day. | **Goedendag.** | Good day. |
| 2 | **Heeft u een bonuskaart?** | Do you have a bonus card? | **Ja, alstublieft.** / **Nee, dank u.** | Yes, here you are. / No, thank you. |
| 3 | **Wilt u een tasje?** | Would you like a bag? | **Ja, graag.** / **Nee, dank u.** | Yes, please. / No, thank you. |
| 4 | **Dat is €12,85.** | That is €12.85. | **Alstublieft.** (tap card or hand cash) | Here you are. |
| 5 | **Pin of contant?** | Card or cash? | **Pin, alstublieft.** | By card, please. |
| 6 | **Wilt u het bonnetje?** | Would you like the receipt? | **Ja, graag.** / **Nee, dank u.** | Yes, please. / No, thank you. |
| 7 | **Statiegeld €0,25.** | Deposit €0.25. | **Oké.** | OK. |
| 8 | **Vriendelijke dag!** | Have a nice day! | **Dank u wel, tot ziens!** | Thank you, goodbye! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Aan de kassa</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Kassière:** Goedemiddag. Heeft u een bonuskaart?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, alstublieft.
    >
    > **Kassière:** Wilt u een tasje? Dat is €0,25.
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, graag.
    >
    > **Kassière:** Dat is €18,40. Pin of contant?
    >
    > **Klant:** Pin, alstublieft.
    >
    > **Kassière:** Wilt u het bonnetje?
    >
    > **Klant:** Nee, dank u. Fijne dag!
    >
    > **Kassière:** Dank u wel!

    **In English:**

    > **Cashier:** Good afternoon. Do you have a bonus card?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, here you are.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Would you like a bag? That is €0.25.
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, please.
    >
    > **Cashier:** That is €18.40. Card or cash?
    >
    > **Customer:** Card, please.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Would you like the receipt?
    >
    > **Customer:** No, thank you. Have a nice day!
    >
    > **Cashier:** Thank you!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
