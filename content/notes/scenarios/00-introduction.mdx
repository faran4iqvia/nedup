## Welcome to Scenarios 🎭

These pages are **not** numbered lessons. They are focused conversation drills for a **specific situation** — before a trip, before an appointment, or before a real conversation you are nervous about.

Each file title shows the level (**A1:**, **A2:**, **B1:**) so you know which grammar and vocabulary it assumes. Study the linked lessons first, then rehearse the scenario aloud.

<Callout title="How to use a scenario page" type="info">
  1. Skim **sentence patterns** so you know the structure.
  2. Read all **key phrases** — staff rarely use every line, but you will recognise them.
  3. Practise answers **out loud**.
  4. Run the **dialogue** twice: once reading, once from memory.
  5. Do the **exercises**, then open **Show answer key**.
  6. Click **Mark lesson complete** when you have rehearsed it once end to end.
</Callout>
