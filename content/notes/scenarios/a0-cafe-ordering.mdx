## Introduction

A Dutch **café** is the perfect place for your first order. Staff are used to tourists; a short Dutch sentence still makes people smile. This scenario uses only **A0** Dutch from [Ordering Food and Drink](/notes/a0/10-ordering-food-and-drink).

<Callout title="Start here" type="info">
  You only need three moves: greet → order → thank you. **Een koffie, alstublieft** is already a full sentence.
</Callout>

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Een koffie, alstublieft.** | A coffee, please. |
| **Ik wil graag …** | I would like … |
| **Mag ik …?** | May I have …? |
| **Dat is € …** | That is € … |
| **Dank u wel.** | Thank you. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| de koffie | coffee |
| de thee | tea |
| het water | water |
| de cappuccino | cappuccino |
| het terras | outdoor terrace |
| warm / koud | hot / cold |
| met melk | with milk |
| zonder suiker | without sugar |
| de rekening | bill |

## Key phrases

| # | Staff | You |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Goedemiddag!** | **Goedemiddag.** |
| 2 | **Wat mag het zijn?** | **Een koffie, alstublieft.** |
| 3 | **Groot of klein?** | **Klein, alstublieft.** |
| 4 | **Met melk?** | **Ja, graag.** / **Nee, zwart.** |
| 5 | **Dat is €3,20.** | **Alstublieft.** (pay) |
| 6 | **Nog iets?** | **Nee, dank u.** |
| 7 | **Mag ik de rekening?** | (when leaving) |
| 8 | **Fijne dag!** | **Dank u wel, tot ziens!** |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Op het terras</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Ober:** Goedemiddag! Wat mag het zijn?
    >
    > **U:** Goedemiddag. Ik wil graag een cappuccino, alstublieft.
    >
    > **Ober:** Met melk en suiker?
    >
    > **U:** Met melk, zonder suiker.
    >
    > **Ober:** Prima. Dat is €3,80.
    >
    > **U:** Alstublieft. Dank u wel!
    >
    > **Ober:** Graag gedaan!

    **In English:**

    > **Server:** Good afternoon! What can I get you?
    >
    > **You:** Good afternoon. I would like a cappuccino, please.
    >
    > **Server:** With milk and sugar?
    >
    > **You:** With milk, without sugar.
    >
    > **Server:** Fine. That is €3.80.
    >
    > **You:** Here you are. Thank you!
    >
    > **Server:** You're welcome!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
