## Introduction

**De bakker** (bakery) is everywhere in the Netherlands. Mornings are busy but the phrases are simple: point, name the item, pay. Builds on [Ordering Food](/notes/a0/10-ordering-food-and-drink) and [de/het](/notes/a0/12-de-het-primer).

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Een …, alstublieft.** | A …, please. |
| **Twee / drie …** | Two / three … |
| **Wat kost het?** / **Hoeveel kost het?** | How much does it cost? |
| **Dat is alles.** | That is everything. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| de bakker / de bakkerij | baker / bakery |
| het brood | bread |
| de croissant | croissant |
| het broodje | bread roll / sandwich |
| de bol | roll (bread) |
| het witte brood | white bread |
| het bruine brood | brown bread |
| vers | fresh |
| gesneden | sliced |

## Key phrases

| # | Baker | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Goedemorgen!** | Good morning! | **Goedemorgen.** | Good morning. |
| 2 | **Wat mag het zijn?** | What can I get you? | **Een bruin broodje, alstublieft.** | A brown roll, please. |
| 3 | **Gesneden?** | Sliced? | **Ja, graag.** | Yes, please. |
| 4 | **Nog iets?** | Anything else? | **En een croissant.** | And a croissant. |
| 5 | **Dat is €4,10.** | That is €4.10. | **Alstublieft.** | Here you are. |
| 6 | **Wilt u een tasje?** | Would you like a bag? | **Ja, alstublieft.** | Yes, please. |
| 7 | **Dank u wel!** | Thank you! | **Dank u wel, fijne dag!** | Thank you, have a nice day! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Bij de bakker</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Bakker:** Goedemorgen! Wat mag het zijn?
    >
    > **Klant:** Goedemorgen. Een wit broodje en twee croissants, alstublieft.
    >
    > **Bakker:** Broodje gesneden?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, graag.
    >
    > **Bakker:** Dat is €5,40.
    >
    > **Klant:** Alstublieft. Dank u wel!
    >
    > **Bakker:** Fijne dag!

    **In English:**

    > **Baker:** Good morning! What can I get you?
    >
    > **Customer:** Good morning. A white roll and two croissants, please.
    >
    > **Baker:** Roll sliced?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, please.
    >
    > **Baker:** That is €5.40.
    >
    > **Customer:** Here you are. Thank you!
    >
    > **Baker:** Have a nice day!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
