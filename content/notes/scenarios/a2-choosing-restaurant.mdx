## Introduction

With friends you compare restaurants: **Dit lijkt me beter**, **goedkoper**, **dichterbij**. Uses [Comparatives](/notes/a2/06-comparatives) and [Opinions](/notes/a2/05-opinions).

## Quick Review

| From your course | You use it here |
| --- | --- |
| [A2 L6](/notes/a2/06-comparatives): **beter**, **dichter** | **Restaurant A is dichterbij.** |
| [A2 L5](/notes/a2/05-opinions): **ik vind** | **Ik vind Italiaans lekkerder.** |
| [A1 Restaurant](/notes/scenarios/a1-restaurant-meal): eating out | **reserveren** |

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Welke lijkt jou beter?** | Which seems better to you? |
| **… is goedkoper / duurder.** | … is cheaper / more expensive. |
| **Ik heb meer zin in …** | I feel more like … |
| **Laten we … kiezen.** | Let's choose … |

## Key phrases

| # | Dutch | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Zullen we vanavond uit eten?** | Shall we eat out tonight? |
| 2 | **De pizzeria is dichterbij en goedkoper.** | The pizzeria is closer and cheaper. |
| 3 | **Ik heb meer zin in curry.** | I feel more like curry. |
| 4 | **Moeten we reserveren?** | Should we book? |
| 5 | **Ik bel ze.** | I will call them. |
| 6 | **Half zeven?** | Half past six? |
| 7 | **Perfect!** | Perfect! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Vanavond eten?</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Anna:** Zullen we vanavond uit eten? Ik zie twee opties: het Thaise restaurant of de pizzeria.
    >
    > **Mark:** De pizzeria is dichterbij en goedkoper, vind ik.
    >
    > **Anna:** Maar het Thaise restaurant heeft betere reviews. Ik heb meer zin in curry.
    >
    > **Mark:** Oké, dan het Thaise. Moeten we reserveren?
    >
    > **Anna:** Ja, ik bel ze. Half zeven?
    >
    > **Mark:** Perfect!

    **In English:**

    > **Anna:** Shall we eat out tonight? I see two options: the Thai restaurant or the pizzeria.
    >
    > **Mark:** The pizzeria is closer and cheaper, I think.
    >
    > **Anna:** But the Thai restaurant has better reviews. I feel more like curry.
    >
    > **Mark:** OK, Thai then. Should we book?
    >
    > **Anna:** Yes, I will call them. Half past six?
    >
    > **Mark:** Perfect!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

## Answer Key

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardContent>
    1. **Restaurant A is goedkoper dan restaurant B.** — Restaurant A is cheaper than restaurant B.
    2. **Welke lijkt jou beter?** / **Wat heeft jouw voorkeur?** — Which seems better to you? / What do you prefer?
    3. **Ik heb meer zin in Italiaans eten.** / **Ik vind Italiaans lekkerder.** — I feel more like Italian food. / I find Italian tastier.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
