## Introduction

**De markt** (street market) is loud, friendly, and great for **A1** practice. Builds on [A0 Asking the Price](/notes/scenarios/a0-asking-the-price) with more food vocabulary from [Food and Drink](/notes/a1/06-food-and-drink).

## Quick Review

| From your course | You use it here |
| --- | --- |
| [A0 Price](/notes/scenarios/a0-asking-the-price): **hoeveel kost** | **Hoeveel kost de kaas?** |
| [A1 L6](/notes/a1/06-food-and-drink): food words | **de appels**, **de kaas** |
| [A1 L8](/notes/a1/08-shopping): **ik neem** | **Ik neem een pond.** |

## Key phrases

| # | Seller | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Goedemorgen! Proeven?** | Good morning! Try some? | **Ja, graag.** | Yes, please. |
| 2 | **Hoeveel wilt u?** | How much would you like? | **Een halve kilo, alstublieft.** | Half a kilo, please. |
| 3 | **Dat is €6.** | That is €6. | **Alstublieft. Dank u wel!** | Here you are. Thank you! |
| 4 | **Nog iets?** | Anything else? | **Nee, dat was alles.** | No, that was everything. |
| 5 | **Fijne dag!** | Have a nice day! | **U ook!** | You too! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Kaas en fruit</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Verkoper:** Goedemorgen! Lekkere oude kaas, proeven?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, graag. … Mmm, lekker. Ik neem 200 gram.
    >
    > **Verkoper:** En appels? Een euro per kilo.
    >
    > **Klant:** Twee kilo, alstublieft. Hoeveel kost het samen?
    >
    > **Verkoper:** €8,40.
    >
    > **Klant:** Alstublieft. Tot volgende week!

    **In English:**

    > **Seller:** Good morning! Tasty aged cheese, try some?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, please. … Mmm, nice. I will take 200 grams.
    >
    > **Seller:** And apples? One euro per kilo.
    >
    > **Customer:** Two kilos, please. How much is it together?
    >
    > **Seller:** €8.40.
    >
    > **Customer:** Here you are. See you next week!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

    2. **Hoeveel kosten de aardbeien?** _How much are the strawberries?_
