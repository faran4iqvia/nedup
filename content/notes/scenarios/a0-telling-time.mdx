## Introduction

Someone says **Uw afspraak is om half drie** — do you know when to arrive? This scenario covers **Hoe laat is het?**, **om … uur**, and reading a simple appointment. Full clock rules (especially **half**) are in [Telling Time](/notes/a0/11-telling-time-and-reading-the-clock).

<Callout title="Dutch half hours" type="info">
  **Half drie** = 2:30, not 3:30. The Dutch **half** points to the _next_ hour. Learn the full rule in A0 Lesson 11 — this scenario gives you the phrases you hear most.
</Callout>

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Hoe laat is het?** | What time is it? |
| **Het is … uur.** | It is … o'clock. |
| **Om … uur** | at … o'clock (appointment) |
| **Half …** | half past (Dutch rule — see lesson) |
| **Kwart over / kwart voor** | quarter past / quarter to |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| het uur | hour / o'clock |
| de minuut | minute |
| hoe laat | what time |
| de afspraak | appointment |
| op tijd | on time |
| te laat | late |
| van … tot … | from … to … |
| morgen / vandaag | tomorrow / today |

## Key phrases

| # | Them | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Hoe laat is het?** | What time is it? | **Het is twee uur.** | It is two o'clock. |
| 2 | **Uw afspraak is om tien uur.** | Your appointment is at ten o'clock. | **Oké, dank u wel.** | OK, thank you. |
| 3 | **Komt u om half tien?** | Are you coming at half past nine? | **Ja, om half tien.** (= 9:30) | Yes, at half past nine. |
| 4 | **We openen om negen uur.** | We open at nine o'clock. | **Dank u.** | Thank you. |
| 5 | **Bent u op tijd?** | Are you on time? | **Ja, ik ben op tijd.** | Yes, I am on time. |
| 6 | **Sorry, ik ben te laat.** | Sorry, I am late. | **Het is vijf minuten.** | It is five minutes. |
| 7 | **Tot morgen om drie uur!** | See you tomorrow at three o'clock! | **Tot morgen!** | See you tomorrow! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Afspraak bij de huisarts</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Receptionist:** Goedemorgen. U heeft een afspraak?
    >
    > **U:** Goedemorgen. Ja, op naam De Vries.
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Uw afspraak is om half elf.
    >
    > **U:** Om half elf … dat is tien uur dertig?
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Ja, klopt. Neem plaats in de wachtkamer.
    >
    > **U:** Dank u wel. Hoe laat is het nu?
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Het is tien uur vijfentwintig. U bent op tijd.
    >
    > **U:** Prima, dank u wel.

    **In English:**

    > **Receptionist:** Good morning. Do you have an appointment?
    >
    > **You:** Good morning. Yes, under the name De Vries.
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Your appointment is at half eleven.
    >
    > **You:** At half eleven … that is ten thirty?
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Yes, correct. Take a seat in the waiting room.
    >
    > **You:** Thank you. What time is it now?
    >
    > **Receptionist:** It is ten twenty-five. You are on time.
    >
    > **You:** Fine, thank you.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
