## Introduction

Dutch trains run on **NS** (Nederlandse Spoorwegen). Most travellers use an **OV-chipkaart** or buy a **los ticket** at a machine or counter. Stations can be busy; these phrases help you buy the right ticket and ask for help. Vocabulary: [Word Hub → OV](/notes/word-hub/nouns#ov-and-public-transport).

Assumes **A1** [Transport](/notes/a1/15-transport) and A0 time/place vocabulary.

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Enkeltje** / **retour** | single / return |
| **Naar welk station?** | To which station? |
| **Welk perron?** | Which platform? |
| **In- en uitchecken** | check in and out |
| **Ik wil graag een ticket naar …** | I would like a ticket to … |

## Key phrases

| # | Staff / machine context | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Waar wilt u naartoe?** | Where would you like to go? | **Naar Den Haag Centraal, alstublieft.** | To Den Haag Centraal, please. |
| 2 | **Enkeltje of retour?** | Single or return? | **Enkeltje.** / **Retour.** | Single. / Return. |
| 3 | **Vandaag of morgen?** | Today or tomorrow? | **Vandaag.** | Today. |
| 4 | **Welk perron naar Utrecht?** | Which platform to Utrecht? | **Perron 7, alstublieft.** | Platform 7, please. |
| 5 | **Is er vertraging?** | Is there a delay? | **Ja, tien minuten.** / **Nee.** | Yes, ten minutes. / No. |
| 6 | **Waar kan ik inchecken?** | Where can I check in? | **Bij de poortjes.** | At the gates. |
| 7 | **Mijn chipkaart werkt niet.** | My OV-chipkaart does not work. | **Naar de servicebalie, alstublieft.** | To the service desk, please. |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Bij NS servicebalie</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Reiziger:** Goedemorgen. Ik wil graag een enkeltje naar Rotterdam Centraal.
    >
    > **Medewerker:** Vandaag?
    >
    > **Reiziger:** Ja, vandaag. Hoeveel kost het?
    >
    > **Medewerker:** €16,80. Pin of contant?
    >
    > **Reiziger:** Pin, alstublieft. Welk perron?
    >
    > **Medewerker:** Perron 4, over vijf minuten.
    >
    > **Reiziger:** Dank u wel!

    **In English:**

    > **Traveller:** Good morning. I would like a single to Rotterdam Central.
    >
    > **Staff:** Today?
    >
    > **Traveller:** Yes, today. How much does it cost?
    >
    > **Staff:** €16.80. Card or cash?
    >
    > **Traveller:** Card, please. Which platform?
    >
    > **Staff:** Platform 4, in five minutes.
    >
    > **Traveller:** Thank you!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
