## Introduction

You are lost or your phone died. Asking **Waar is …?** and understanding **rechts**, **links**, and **rechtdoor** gets you there. Dutch people often switch to English, but these phrases work when they answer in Dutch.

Assumes **A1** [Asking and Giving Directions](/notes/a1/23-asking-and-giving-directions) and [Transport](/notes/a1/15-transport). This scenario is the drill for that lesson.

## Quick Review

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Waar is** …? | Where is …? |
| **Hoe kom ik bij** …? | How do I get to …? |
| **Is het ver?** | Is it far? |
| **Ga rechtdoor. Sla linksaf.** | Go straight ahead. Turn left. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| rechtdoor | straight ahead |
| links / linksaf | left / turn left |
| rechts / rechtsaf | right / turn right |
| de hoek | corner |
| om de hoek | around the corner |
| tegenover | opposite |
| naast | next to |
| het kruispunt | intersection |
| lopen | walk |
| de brug | bridge |

## Key phrases

| # | You ask | English | They answer | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Excuseer, waar is het station?** | Excuse me, where is the station? | **Rechtdoor, dan links.** | Straight ahead, then left. |
| 2 | **Hoe kom ik bij de supermarkt?** | How do I get to the supermarket? | **Om de hoek, naast de bank.** | Around the corner, next to the bank. |
| 3 | **Is het ver lopen?** | Is it far to walk? | **Nee, twee minuten.** | No, two minutes. |
| 4 | **Moet ik rechts of links?** | Should I go right or left? | **Rechts, bij het stoplicht.** | Right, at the traffic lights. |
| 5 | **Bedankt!** | Thanks! | **Geen probleem!** | No problem! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Op straat</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Tourist:** Excuseer, hoe kom ik bij het Rijksmuseum?
    >
    > **Passant:** Ga rechtdoor tot het kruispunt. Sla dan rechtsaf. Het museum is aan je linkerhand.
    >
    > **Tourist:** Is het ver?
    >
    > **Passant:** Nee, ongeveer tien minuten lopen.
    >
    > **Tourist:** Dank u wel!
    >
    > **Passant:** Graag gedaan!

    **In English:**

    > **Tourist:** Excuse me, how do I get to the Rijksmuseum?
    >
    > **Passer-by:** Go straight until the crossing. Then turn right. The museum is on your left.
    >
    > **Tourist:** Is it far?
    >
    > **Passer-by:** No, about ten minutes on foot.
    >
    > **Tourist:** Thank you!
    >
    > **Passer-by:** You're welcome!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
