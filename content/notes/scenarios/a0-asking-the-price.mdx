## Introduction

Before you buy, you ask **Hoeveel kost het?** (How much does it cost?). This works at a **markt**, souvenir shop, or **kiosk**. From [Numbers and Money](/notes/a0/07-numbers-21-100-and-money) and [Basic Questions](/notes/a0/06-basic-questions).

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Hoeveel kost het?** | How much does it cost? |
| **Wat kost …?** | What does … cost? |
| **Dat is € …** | That is € … |
| **Dat is te duur.** | That is too expensive. |
| **Oké, ik neem het.** | OK, I will take it. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| hoeveel | how much |
| kosten | to cost |
| duur / goedkoop | expensive / cheap |
| de euro | euro |
| cent | cent |
| de markt | market |
| de winkel | shop |
| ik neem … | I will take … |
| samen | together (total) |

## Key phrases

| # | Seller | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Goedemiddag!** | Good afternoon! | **Goedemiddag.** | Good afternoon. |
| 2 | **Kan ik u helpen?** | Can I help you? | **Hoeveel kost deze kaart?** | How much does this card cost? |
| 3 | **Dat is drie euro.** | That is three euros. | **Oké, ik neem hem.** | OK, I will take it. |
| 4 | — | — | **Hoeveel kost het samen?** | How much is it together? (two items) |
| 5 | **Dat is acht euro vijftig.** | That is eight euros fifty. | **Alstublieft.** | Here you are. |
| 6 | **Wilt u pinnen of contant?** | Would you like to pay by card or cash? | **Pin, alstublieft.** | By card, please. |
| 7 | **Dank u wel!** | Thank you! | **Dank u wel, tot ziens!** | Thank you, goodbye! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Op de markt</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Verkoper:** Goedemorgen!
    >
    > **Klant:** Goedemorgen. Hoeveel kosten de appels?
    >
    > **Verkoper:** Twee euro per kilo.
    >
    > **Klant:** Ik neem een halve kilo, alstublieft.
    >
    > **Verkoper:** Dat is een euro. Nog iets?
    >
    > **Klant:** Hoeveel kost deze kaas?
    >
    > **Verkoper:** Vijf euro.
    >
    > **Klant:** Oké. Hoeveel kost het samen?
    >
    > **Verkoper:** Zes euro.
    >
    > **Klant:** Alstublieft. Dank u wel!

    **In English:**

    > **Seller:** Good morning!
    >
    > **Customer:** Good morning. How much are the apples?
    >
    > **Seller:** Two euros per kilo.
    >
    > **Customer:** I will take half a kilo, please.
    >
    > **Seller:** That is one euro. Anything else?
    >
    > **Customer:** How much is this cheese?
    >
    > **Seller:** Five euros.
    >
    > **Customer:** OK. How much is it together?
    >
    > **Seller:** Six euros.
    >
    > **Customer:** Here you are. Thank you!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
