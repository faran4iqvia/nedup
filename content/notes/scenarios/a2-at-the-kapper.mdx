## Introduction

Time for a **knipbeurt** at **de kapper** or **de kapsalon**. Dutch hairdressers are friendly and often use informal **je** once you sit down — but you can always stay polite with **u** if you prefer.

This scenario assumes **A1** basics plus **A2** description from [Describing People](/notes/a2/08-describing-people) and [Opinions](/notes/a2/05-opinions): **iets korter**, **niet te veel eraf**, **ik wil graag …**.

<Callout title="Tip" type="info">
  Bring a **foto** on your phone if you are nervous. Say **een beetje** (a little) — Dutch hairdressers often cut less than you expect, which is safer than **veel korter**!
</Callout>

## Grammar and Language Explanation

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik wil graag** … | I would like … |
| **Een beetje** + comparative | A little …, please. |
| **Niet te** … | Not too … |
| **Kunt u** …? | Can you …? |
| **Hoeveel kost** …? | How much does … cost? |

### Services

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **knippen** | to cut (hair) |
| **wassen en knippen** | wash and cut |
| **alleen de puntjes** | just the ends / trim |
| **fohnen** | to blow-dry |
| **verven / kleuren** | to dye / colour |
| **de baard trimmen** | to trim the beard |
| **met pony** | with bangs |

## Key phrases

| # | Kapper | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Hoi! Heb je een afspraak?** | Hi! Do you have an appointment? | **Ja, op naam Lisa, om twee uur.** | Yes, under the name Lisa, at two o'clock. |
| 2 | **Wat wil je vandaag?** | What would you like today? | **Knippen, alstublieft. Een beetje korter.** | A cut, please. A bit shorter. |
| 3 | **Hoeveel eraf?** | How much off? | **Ongeveer twee centimeter.** | About two centimetres. |
| 4 | **Wil je wassen?** | Do you want a wash? | **Ja, graag.** / **Nee, het is al gewassen.** | Yes, please. / No, it is already washed. |
| 5 | **Zo goed?** | Like this? | **Ja, perfect.** / **Nog iets korter aan de zijkant.** | Yes, perfect. / A bit shorter on the sides. |
| 6 | **Wil je föhnen?** | Do you want a blow-dry? | **Ja, alstublieft.** | Yes, please. |
| 7 | **Dat is €28.** | That is €28. | **Kan ik pinnen?** | Can I pay by card? |
| 8 | **Tot de volgende keer!** | See you next time! | **Dank je wel, tot ziens!** | Thank you, goodbye! |

## Example Conversations

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Walk-in — knippen</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Kapper:** Hoi, welkom! Heb je een afspraak of kom je zonder?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ik heb om drie uur een afspraak. Naam Bakker.
    >
    > **Kapper:** Prima, ga zitten. Wat wil je vandaag?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ik wil graag knippen. Het is te lang — een beetje korter, maar niet te veel.
    >
    > **Kapper:** Ongeveer een paar centimeter?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, en de puntjes. Aan de voorkant niet te kort.
    >
    > **Kapper:** Oké. Wil je eerst wassen?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, graag.
    >
    > _(later)_
    >
    > **Kapper:** Zo? Wil je de spiegel?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, ziet er goed uit. Dank je wel!
    >
    > **Kapper:** Mooi! Dat is €26. Pin of contant?
    >
    > **Klant:** Pin, alstublieft.

    **In English:**

    > **Hairdresser:** Hi, welcome! Do you have an appointment or walk-in?
    >
    > **Customer:** I have an appointment at three. Name Bakker.
    >
    > **Hairdresser:** Fine, take a seat. What would you like today?
    >
    > **Customer:** I would like a cut. It is too long — a bit shorter, but not too much.
    >
    > **Hairdresser:** About a few centimetres?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, and the ends. Not too short at the front.
    >
    > **Hairdresser:** OK. Do you want a wash first?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, please.
    >
    > _(later)_
    >
    > **Hairdresser:** Like this? Want the mirror?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, looks good. Thank you!
    >
    > **Hairdresser:** Nice! That is €26. Card or cash?
    >
    > **Customer:** Card, please.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
