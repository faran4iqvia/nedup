## Introduction

In the Netherlands your **huisarts** (GP) is the first contact for non emergency health issues. Appointments are usually required. This scenario covers phone and desk phrases at **de huisartsenpraktijk**.

Assumes **A1** [Body and Health](/notes/a1/17-body-health) and [A2 Appointments](/notes/a2/19-appointments) patterns preview (**de afspraak**).

<Callout title="Emergency" type="info">
  Life threatening emergency: call **112**. For urgent but not life threatening care outside GP hours, use **huisartsenpost** (GP post).
</Callout>

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik wil graag een afspraak maken.** | I would like to make an appointment. |
| **Ik ben ziek / verkouden.** | I am ill / have a cold. |
| **Sinds wanneer?** | Since when? (e.g. **Sinds maandag.** — Since Monday.) |
| **Heeft u nog klachten?** | Do you still have symptoms? |

## Key phrases (phone)

| # | Reception | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Huisartsenpraktijk De Vries, goedemorgen.** | De Vries GP practice, good morning. | **Goedemorgen. Ik wil graag een afspraak maken.** | Good morning. I would like to make an appointment. |
| 2 | **Wat is uw naam en geboortedatum?** | What is your name and date of birth? | **Ik heet Jan Bakker. 12 maart 1990.** | My name is Jan Bakker. 12 March 1990. |
| 3 | **Wat zijn uw klachten?** | What are your symptoms? | **Ik heb koorts en hoest sinds gisteren.** | I have had a fever and cough since yesterday. |
| 4 | **Kan het morgen?** | Can it be tomorrow? | **Ja, morgenochtend graag.** | Yes, tomorrow morning please. |
| 5 | **Half tien is goed?** | Is half past nine OK? | **Prima, dank u wel.** | Fine, thank you. |
| 6 | **Neem uw verzekeringspas mee.** | Bring your insurance card. | **Oké, dank u wel. Tot morgen.** | OK, thank you. See you tomorrow. |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Telefoon — afspraak maken</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Assistente:** Goedemiddag, huisartsenpraktijk Bakker.
    >
    > **Patiënt:** Goedemiddag. Ik wil graag een afspraak. Ik ben verkouden en heb keelpijn.
    >
    > **Assistente:** Sinds wanneer?
    >
    > **Patiënt:** Sinds drie dagen.
    >
    > **Assistente:** Heeft u koorts?
    >
    > **Patiënt:** Ja, een beetje.
    >
    > **Assistente:** Morgen om elf uur?
    >
    > **Patiënt:** Dat is goed. Dank u wel!
    >
    > **Assistente:** Tot morgen. Beterschap!

    **In English:**

    > **Receptionist:** Good afternoon, Bakker GP practice.
    >
    > **Patient:** Good afternoon. I would like an appointment. I have a cold and sore throat.
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Since when?
    >
    > **Patient:** For three days.
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Do you have a fever?
    >
    > **Patient:** Yes, a little.
    >
    > **Receptionist:** Tomorrow at eleven?
    >
    > **Patient:** That works. Thank you!
    >
    > **Receptionist:** See you tomorrow. Get well soon!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
