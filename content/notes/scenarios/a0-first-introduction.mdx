## Introduction

Your first Dutch conversation is often an introduction: at work, in class, or with a neighbour. You only need **ik ben**, **ik heet**, and **aangenaam**. From [Greetings](/notes/a0/01-greetings-and-introductions) and [Nationalities](/notes/a0/13-nationalities-and-languages).

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Hallo, ik ben …** | Hello, I am … |
| **Ik heet …** | My name is … |
| **Aangenaam.** / **Leuk je te ontmoeten.** | Nice to meet you. |
| **Hoe heet u?** | What is your name? (formal) |
| **Waar komt u vandaan?** | Where are you from? |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| hallo / goedendag | hello |
| ik ben / ik heet | I am / my name is |
| aangenaam | pleased to meet you |
| de naam | name |
| uit … komen | to come from |
| de buurman / de buurvrouw | neighbour (m/f) |
| de collega | colleague |
| spreken | to speak |

## Key phrases

| # | Them | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Hallo!** | Hello! | **Hallo!** | Hello! |
| 2 | **Ik ben Mark. En jij?** | I am Mark. And you? | **Ik heet Priya. Aangenaam.** | My name is Priya. Nice to meet you. |
| 3 | **Waar kom je vandaan?** | Where are you from? | **Ik kom uit Londen.** | I come from London. |
| 4 | **Spreek je Nederlands?** | Do you speak Dutch? | **Een beetje. Ik leer Nederlands.** | A little. I am learning Dutch. |
| 5 | **Welkom in Nederland!** | Welcome to the Netherlands! | **Dank je wel!** | Thank you! |
| 6 | **Hoe heet u?** (formal) | What is your name? (formal) | **Ik heet Bakker. Aangenaam.** | My name is Bakker. Nice to meet you. |
| 7 | **Tot ziens!** | Goodbye! | **Tot ziens!** | Goodbye! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Nieuwe buurvrouw</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Buurvrouw:** Hallo! Ik ben Emma. Jij bent de nieuwe buurman?
    >
    > **U:** Hallo! Ja, ik ben nieuw hier. Ik heet Sam.
    >
    > **Buurvrouw:** Aangenaam, Sam! Waar kom je vandaan?
    >
    > **U:** Ik kom uit Canada. En jij?
    >
    > **Buurvrouw:** Uit Utrecht. Spreek je al Nederlands?
    >
    > **U:** Een beetje. Ik leer het nu.
    >
    > **Buurvrouw:** Leuk! Welkom. Tot later!
    >
    > **U:** Dank je wel. Tot ziens!

    **In English:**

    > **Neighbour:** Hello! I am Emma. You are the new neighbour?
    >
    > **You:** Hello! Yes, I am new here. My name is Sam.
    >
    > **Neighbour:** Nice to meet you, Sam! Where are you from?
    >
    > **You:** I come from Canada. And you?
    >
    > **Neighbour:** From Utrecht. Do you already speak Dutch?
    >
    > **You:** A little. I am learning it now.
    >
    > **Neighbour:** Nice! Welcome. See you later!
    >
    > **You:** Thank you. Goodbye!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

## Answer Key

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardContent>
    1. **Hallo, ik heet Anna. Aangenaam.** _Hello, my name is Anna. Nice to meet you._
    2. **Ik kom uit …** (your country) _I come from …_
    3. Sample: **Goedemiddag. Ik heet De Vries. Hoe heet u?** — **Ik heet Jansen. Aangenaam.** _Good afternoon. My name is De Vries. What is your name?_ — _My name is Jansen. Nice to meet you._
  </CardContent>
