## Introduction

**Op kantoor** you hear **Hoe was je weekend?** and **Zullen we lunchen?** Uses [Workplace](/notes/a2/17-workplace) and [Opinions](/notes/a2/05-opinions).

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Hoe was je weekend?** | How was your weekend? |
| **Lekker rustig / druk.** | Nice and quiet / busy. |
| **Wat ga je lunch eten?** | What are you going to eat for lunch? |
| **Zullen we samen lunchen?** | Shall we have lunch together? |
| **Ik moet even een meeting in.** | I have to go into a meeting. |

## Key phrases

| # | Dutch | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Hoe was je weekend?** | How was your weekend? |
| 2 | **Ik ben naar de Efteling geweest met mijn kinderen.** | I went to the Efteling with my children. |
| 3 | **Was het druk?** | Was it busy? |
| 4 | **Ja, best druk, maar we hebben veel gelachen.** | Yes, quite busy, but we laughed a lot. |
| 5 | **Ik heb thuis geklust.** | I did DIY at home. |
| 6 | **Zullen we straks samen lunchen?** | Shall we have lunch together later? |
| 7 | **Om twaalf uur?** | At twelve o'clock? |
| 8 | **Prima!** | Fine! |

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Maandagochtend — koffieautomaat</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Collega:** Hé, goedemorgen! Hoe was je weekend?
    >
    > **U:** Goedemorgen! Lekker. Ik ben naar de Efteling geweest met mijn kinderen.
    >
    > **Collega:** Leuk! Was het druk?
    >
    > **U:** Ja, best druk, maar we hebben veel gelachen. En jij?
    >
    > **Collega:** Rustig. Ik heb thuis geklust. Zullen we straks samen lunchen?
    >
    > **U:** Goed idee. Om twaalf uur?
    >
    > **Collega:** Prima!

    **In English:**

    > **Colleague:** Hey, good morning! How was your weekend?
    >
    > **You:** Good morning! Nice. I went to the Efteling with my children.
    >
    > **Colleague:** Fun! Was it busy?
    >
    > **You:** Yes, quite busy, but we laughed a lot. And you?
    >
    > **Colleague:** Quiet. I did DIY at home. Shall we have lunch together later?
    >
    > **You:** Good idea. At twelve o'clock?
    >
    > **Colleague:** Fine!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

## Answer Key

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardContent>
    1. Sample: **Lekker! Ik heb vrienden bezocht.** / **Rustig, ik heb uitgerust.** — Nice! I visited friends. / Quiet, I rested.
    2. **Zullen we samen lunchen?** / **Heb je zin om te lunchen?** — Shall we have lunch together? / Do you feel like having lunch?
    3. **Ik heb om één uur een meeting.** / **Ik moet om één uur naar een vergadering.** — I have a meeting at one o'clock. / I have to go to a meeting at one o'clock.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
