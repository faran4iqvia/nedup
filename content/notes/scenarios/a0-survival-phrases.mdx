## Introduction

The most useful **A0** sentences are not about grammar — they keep a conversation alive when you are lost. This scenario collects phrases from [Getting By: Survival Phrases](/notes/a0/14-getting-by-survival-phrases). Learn them until they are automatic.

<Callout title="Use these without shame" type="info">
  Dutch people appreciate that you try. **Kunt u dat langzamer zeggen?** is normal and polite — not rude.
</Callout>

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik begrijp het niet.** | I do not understand (it). |
| **Kunt u dat herhalen?** | Can you repeat that? |
| **Langzaam, alstublieft.** | Slowly, please. |
| **Wat betekent …?** | What does … mean? |
| **Spreekt u Engels?** | Do you speak English? |
| **Ik leer Nederlands.** | I am learning Dutch. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| begrijpen | to understand |
| herhalen | to repeat |
| langzaam | slowly |
| betekenen | to mean |
| het woord | word |
| even | just / for a moment |
| sorry / pardon | sorry / excuse me |
| langzaam praten | to speak slowly |

## Key phrases

| # | Situation | You say |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Too fast | **Sorry, ik begrijp het niet.** |
| 2 | Repeat | **Kunt u dat herhalen?** |
| 3 | Slow down | **Kunt u langzamer praten, alstublieft?** |
| 4 | One word | **Wat betekent dat?** |
| 5 | Spelling | **Hoe spelt u dat?** |
| 6 | English | **Spreekt u Engels?** |
| 7 | Still learning | **Ik spreek een beetje Nederlands.** |
| 8 | Thanks for help | **Dank u wel, u spreekt duidelijk.** |

## Common Mistakes

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Missing het</CardTitle></CardHeader>
  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik begrijp niet.** · ✅ **Ik begrijp het niet.** · Same for **Ik weet het niet.**
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Kunt u vs Kun je</CardTitle></CardHeader>
  <CardContent>
    **Kunt u** = formal (shop, doctor, stranger) · **Kun je** = informal (friend, young kapper) · Both are fine when learning
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Bij de supermarkt — te snel</CardTitle></CardHeader>
  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Kassière:** Goedemiddag! Bonuskaart en een tasje en dat is €12,40 pin of contant bonnetje?
    >
    > **U:** Sorry, ik begrijp het niet. Kunt u dat herhalen?
    >
    > **Kassière:** *(langzamer)* Heeft u een bonuskaart?
    >
    > **U:** Nee, dank u.
    >
    > **Kassière:** Wilt u een tasje? Dat is €12,40. Pin of contant?
    >
    > **U:** Pin, alstublieft. Dank u wel. Ik leer Nederlands.
    >
    > **Kassière:** Goed bezig!

    **In English:**

    > **Cashier:** Good afternoon! Bonus card and a bag and that is €12.40 card or cash receipt?
    >
    > **You:** Sorry, I do not understand. Can you repeat that?
    >
    > **Cashier:** *(slower)* Do you have a bonus card?
    >
    > **You:** No, thank you.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Would you like a bag? That is €12.40. Card or cash?
    >
    > **You:** Card, please. Thank you. I am learning Dutch.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Well done!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
