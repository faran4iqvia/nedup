## Introduction

After **Hallo, ik heet …** people often ask **Waar woon je?** or **Waar kom je vandaan?** Short answers with **in** and **uit** are enough at **A0**. From [Where Do You Live](/notes/a0/09-where-do-you-live).

## Quick Review

| From your course | You use it here |
| --- | --- |
| [A0 L9](/notes/a0/09-where-do-you-live): **ik woon in** | **Ik woon in Amsterdam.** |
| [A0 L13](/notes/a0/13-nationalities-and-languages): **ik kom uit** | **Ik kom uit Polen.** |

## Key patterns

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Waar woon je?** | Where do you live? |
| **Ik woon in …** | I live in … |
| **Waar kom je vandaan?** | Where are you from? |
| **Ik kom uit …** | I come from … |
| **Ik ben nieuw hier.** | I am new here. |
| **In Nederland.** | In the Netherlands. |

## Core Vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| wonen | to live |
| de stad | city |
| het land | country |
| Nederland | the Netherlands |
| Amsterdam / Rotterdam / Utrecht | cities |
| hier | here |
| nieuw | new |
| de buurt | neighbourhood |

## Key phrases

| # | Them | You |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Waar woon je?** | **Ik woon in Den Haag.** |
| 2 | **Woon je in Nederland?** | **Ja, ik woon in Nederland.** |
| 3 | **Waar kom je vandaan?** | **Ik kom uit Brazilië.** |
| 4 | **Hoe lang woon je hier?** | **Twee maanden.** |
| 5 | **Ik ben net verhuisd.** | I just moved. |
| 6 | **Leuk hier!** | Nice here! |
| 7 | **En jij?** | And you? (return question) |

## Common Mistakes

  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">in vs uit</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **woon in** = you live there now · **kom uit** = origin/nationality · ✅ **Ik woon in Utrecht. Ik kom uit Turkije.**
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Ik woon uit …</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    ❌ **Ik woon uit India.** · ✅ **Ik kom uit India.** · ✅ **Ik woon in India.** (if you still live there)
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

## Example Conversation

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader><CardTitle className="text-lg">Taalles — na de les</CardTitle></CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **In Dutch:**

    > **Klasgenoot:** Hoi! Waar woon jij?
    >
    > **U:** Hoi! Ik woon in Eindhoven. En jij?
    >
    > **Klasgenoot:** Ik woon in Eindhoven ook, bij het station.
    >
    > **U:** Leuk! Waar kom jij vandaan?
    >
    > **Klasgenoot:** Uit Italië. En jij?
    >
    > **U:** Ik kom uit Egypte. Ik woon pas drie maanden in Nederland.
    >
    > **Klasgenoot:** Welkom! Spreek je al goed Nederlands?
    >
    > **U:** Een beetje. Ik leer het nu.
    >
    > **Klasgenoot:** Tot de volgende les!

    **In English:**

    > **Classmate:** Hi! Where do you live?
    >
    > **You:** Hi! I live in Eindhoven. And you?
    >
    > **Classmate:** I live in Eindhoven too, near the station.
    >
    > **You:** Nice! Where are you from?
    >
    > **Classmate:** From Italy. And you?
    >
    > **You:** I come from Egypt. I have only lived in the Netherlands for three months.
    >
    > **Classmate:** Welcome! Do you already speak Dutch well?
    >
    > **You:** A little. I am learning it now.
    >
    > **Classmate:** See you next lesson!
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>
