## Introduction

At **het museum**, **de bioscoop**, or a tourist desk you buy **een kaartje**. Simple questions: time, price, how many people. Uses [A0 Numbers](/notes/a0/07-numbers-21-100-and-money) and [A1 Shopping](/notes/a1/08-shopping).

## Key phrases

| # | Staff | English | You | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Goedemiddag. Waarmee kan ik u helpen?** | Good afternoon. How can I help you? | **Twee kaartjes voor het museum, alstublieft.** | Two tickets for the museum, please. |
| 2 | **Volwassene of kind?** | Adult or child? | **Twee volwassenen.** | Two adults. |
| 3 | **Voor vandaag om drie uur?** | For today at three o'clock? | **Ja, dat klopt.** | Yes, that is correct. |
| 4 | **Dat is €22.** | That is €22. | **Pin, alstublieft.** | By card, please. |
| 5 | **Hier zijn uw kaartjes. Veel plezier!** | Here are your tickets. Enjoy! | **Dank u wel!** | Thank you! |

    > **Kassamedewerker:** Goedenavond! Welke film?
    >
    > **Klant:** **Dune**, om acht uur, alstublieft.

    > **Kassamedewerker:** Twee kaartjes? Volwassene?
    >
    > **Klant:** Ja, twee volwassenen.

    > **Kassamedewerker:** Zaaldrie. Dat is €26. Wilt u popcorn?
    >
    > **Klant:** Nee, dank u. Alleen de kaartjes.
    >
    > **Kassamedewerker:** Alstublieft. Fijne film!

    **In English:**

    > **Cashier:** Good evening! Which film?
    >
    > **Customer:** **Dune**, at eight o'clock, please.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Two tickets? Adult?
    >
    > **Customer:** Yes, two adults.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Hall three. That is €26. Would you like popcorn?
    >
    > **Customer:** No, thank you. Just the tickets.
    >
    > **Cashier:** Here you are. Enjoy the film!
  </CardContent>
