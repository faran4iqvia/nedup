## Introduction

Dutch has no single future tense like English _will_. B1 learners combine **gaan** + infinitive, **zullen** + infinitive, and **present tense** for fixed schedules. Main lesson: [B1 Lesson 4](/notes/b1/04-future-with-zullen).

Not sure which past, passive, or future form? See [Tense and Register Choice](/notes/grammar/tense-and-register-choice).

## Explanation

### Three future strategies

| Strategy | Pattern | Typical use | English example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Gaan** + infinitive | Ik **ga** morgen **werken** | near, concrete plan | I am going to work tomorrow. |
| **Zullen** + infinitive | Ik **zal** je **bellen** | promise, prediction, formal | I will call you. |
| **Present tense** | De trein **vertrekt** om acht uur | timetables, programs | The train leaves at eight. |

### zullen conjugation

| ik | jij/u | hij/zij/het | wij/jullie/zij |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **zal** | **zult** / **zal** | **zal** | **zullen** |

### zullen uses

| Use | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Promise | Ik **zal** je **helpen**. |
| Suggestion | **Zullen** we **afspreken**? |
| Prediction | Het **zal** morgen **regenen**. |
| Soft guess | Hij **zal** wel **moe** zijn. |
| Formal announcement | Het college **zal** plaatsvinden. |

### gaan + infinitive

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| Ik **ga** straks **bellen**. | I am going to call shortly. |
| Het **gaat** **regenen**. | It is going to rain. |
| Wij **gaan** volgende week **verhuizen**. | We are moving next week. |

### Present for future (schedules)

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| De vergadering **begint** om tien uur. | The meeting starts at ten. |
| Het vliegtuig **vertrekt** morgen. | The plane departs tomorrow. |

### zullen in subordinate clauses

Verb goes to the **end**: **Ik denk dat hij zal komen**.

## Quick Reference

| Meaning | Form |
| --- | --- |
| Near plan | **gaan** + infinitive |
| Promise / prediction | **zullen** + infinitive |
| Timetable | present tense |
| Suggestion | **Zullen** we …? |
