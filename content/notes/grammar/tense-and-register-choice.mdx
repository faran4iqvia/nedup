## Explanation

### Past: perfect vs simple past

| Situation | Prefer | Dutch example | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Spoken update, chat, news you lived | **perfect** | **Ik heb gisteren gewerkt.** | I worked yesterday. |
| Narrative, fairy tales, written sequence | **simple past** | **Ik werkte de hele dag.** | I worked all day. |
| Story opener with **toen** | **simple past** | **Toen ik aankwam, was hij weg.** | When I arrived, he was gone. |
| Result still relevant now | **perfect** | **Ik ben naar Amsterdam geweest.** | I have been to Amsterdam. |

Full rules: [18: Perfect](/notes/grammar/18-perfect-tense) ┬Ě [16](/notes/grammar/16-simple-past-regular) ┬Ě [17](/notes/grammar/17-simple-past-irregular) ┬Ě [52: Storytelling](/notes/grammar/52-storytelling-past-tenses).

### Passive: wordt vs is

| Meaning | Form | Dutch | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| In progress, ongoing process | **worden** + participle | **De brug wordt gebouwd.** | The bridge is being built. |
| Completed state, result | **zijn** + participle | **De brug is gebouwd.** | The bridge is built. |
| English "is being" | **wordt**, not **is** | **Het huis wordt gerenoveerd.** | The house is being renovated. |
| Impersonal rule | **Er wordt** ÔÇŽ | **Er wordt niet gerookt.** | Smoking is not allowed. |

Full rules: [30: Passive intro](/notes/grammar/30-passive-voice) ┬Ě [38: worden](/notes/grammar/38-passive-with-worden) ┬Ě [39: zijn](/notes/grammar/39-passive-with-zijn).

### Future: gaan, zullen, present

| Meaning | Form | Dutch | English |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Near concrete plan | **gaan** + infinitive | **Ik ga morgen werken.** | I'm going to work tomorrow. |
| Promise, prediction, formal | **zullen** + infinitive | **Ik zal je bellen.** | I will call you. |
| Timetable, schedule | **present** | **De trein vertrekt om acht uur.** | The train leaves at eight. |
| Suggestion | **Zullen we** ÔÇŽ? | **Zullen we afspreken?** | Shall we meet up? |

Full rules: [34: Future](/notes/grammar/34-future-tense) ┬Ě [35: zou](/notes/grammar/35-zou-politeness-and-advice) (softened **zullen**).

### Spoken vs written (summary)

| Feature | Spoken / informal | Written / formal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Past events | perfect | simple past or perfect |
| Passive | active often OK | **wordt** / **is** passives common |
| Future | **gaan** | **zullen**, present schedules |
| Relative + prep. | **die ÔÇŽ over** at end | **waarover** ([33](/notes/grammar/33-waar-plus-preposition)) |

Register tone ( **jij** vs **u**, email frames): [Register and Formality](/notes/grammar/register-and-formality).
