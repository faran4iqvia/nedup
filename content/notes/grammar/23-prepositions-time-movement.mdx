## Introduction

Dutch uses different prepositions for **where things go** and **when things happen**. Main lesson: [A2 Lesson 11](/notes/phase-2-a1-a2/a2/11-prepositions-time). Static place: [Grammar 22](/notes/grammar/22-prepositions-place).

## Explanation

### Movement

| Preposition | Meaning | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **naar** | to / towards | naar Amsterdam |
| **van** | from | van Utrecht |
| **door** | through | door de tunnel |
| **langs** | along | langs de rivier |
| **uit** | out of / from (origin) | uit Nederland |
| **met** | by (transport) | met de trein |
| **te voet** | on foot | te voet |

**Van … naar …** · **Van … tot …** (place or time)

### Clock time

| Preposition | Use | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **om** | at (clock) | om acht uur |
| **van … tot …** | from … to … | van negen tot vijf |
| **tot** | until | tot vrijdag |
| **sinds** | since | sinds maandag |
| **tijdens** | during | tijdens de les |
| **voor** | before | voor het eten |
| **na** | after | na de vergadering |
| **vanaf** | from (forward) | vanaf morgen |

### Days, months, seasons

| Preposition | Use | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **op** | days, dates | op maandag, op 5 juni |
| **in** | months, seasons, years | in mei, in de zomer, in 2024 |
| **in** | parts of day | in de ochtend |
| **'s ochtends** etc. | short forms | 's avonds |

### Approximate / deadline

**omstreeks** · **rond** · **tegen** · **uiterlijk**

### Static vs movement

| Static (L10) | Movement (L11) |
| --- | --- |
| Ik ben **in** de winkel. | Ik ga **naar** de winkel. |

### sinds + tense

Continuing situation: **Ik woon hier sinds 2020** (present).

## Quick Reference

| Category | Prepositions |
| --- | --- |
| Movement | naar, van, door, uit, langs |
| Clock | om, van … tot, tot, sinds |
| Calendar | op (days), in (months/seasons) |
| During / before / after | tijdens, voor, na, vanaf |
