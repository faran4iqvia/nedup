## Introduction

You know **studeren** from A1 and **slagen voor het examen** from A2. B1 adds the **Dutch education system**, university life, and lifelong learning. Main lesson: [B1 Lesson 18](/notes/b1/18-education-and-learning).

## Explanation

### Dutch school and study tracks

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **de** **basisschool** | primary school |
| **de** **middelbare** **school** | secondary school |
| **het** **vwo** / **havo** / **mbo** | academic / general / vocational track |
| **de** **hogeschool** | university of applied sciences |
| **de** **universiteit** | research university |
| **de** **opleiding** | course of study / programme |

### Passing and failing

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **slagen** **voor** | to pass |
| **zakken** **voor** | to fail |
| **geslaagd** | passed |
| **gezakt** | failed |

### University vocabulary

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **het** **college** / **hoorcollege** | lecture |
| **het** **werkcollege** | tutorial / seminar |
| **het** **tentamen** | exam (university) |
| **de** **scriptie** | thesis (bachelor level) |
| **het** **studiepunt** | credit point |

### Study purpose with om te and te

From [A2 om te](/notes/grammar/25-om-te-infinitive) and [B1 te](/notes/grammar/41-te-plus-infinitive):

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| **om** **te** **leren** | Ik **ga** **naar** **de** **bibliotheek** **om** **te** **studeren**. |
| **door** **te** **oefenen** | Je **leert** **Nederlands** **door** **te** **oefenen**. |
| **na** **het** **volgen** **van** | **Na** **het** **volgen** **van** **de** **cursus** … |

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

❌ **Ik** **ben** **geslaagd** **voor** **het** **examen**. (sometimes heard)

✅ **Ik** **ben** **geslaagd**. / **Ik** **heb** **het** **examen** **gehaald**.

**Geslaagd** often stands alone. **Slagen voor** + exam.

❌ **Ik** **studeer** **op** **de** **universiteit**.

✅ **Ik** **studeer** **aan** **de** **universiteit**. / **Ik** **zit** **op** **de** **universiteit**.

Use **aan** with **studeren** at an institution.
