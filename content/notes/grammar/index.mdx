## Welcome to the Grammar Track 💡

This is the reference shelf: every core grammar topic from A0 through B1 in
one place, ordered from simplest to most advanced. Use it alongside the level
courses whenever you want the full picture of a topic.

**What's covered:**

- Pronouns, *zijn*, *hebben*, and the present tense
- Questions, word order, and negation
- Articles (*de* / *het*), possessives, demonstratives, plurals, and adjectives
- Reflexive and separable verbs
- Past tenses: simple past, perfect tense, weak and strong verbs
- Modal verbs, comparatives, and prepositions
- Complex sentences: relative clauses, *om te*, *want* / *omdat*
- *Er* constructions, conditionals, and the passive voice

Start with [Personal Pronouns](/notes/grammar/01-personal-pronouns).
