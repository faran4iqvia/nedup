## Explanation

Main clause: **Subject · Verb · Rest**.

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| Ik **werk** vandaag. | I work today. |
| **Vandaag** werk ik. | Today I work. |
| Ik **koop** brood **in de winkel**. | I buy bread in the shop. |

If something other than the subject starts the sentence, the verb stays second.

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Morgen** ga ik naar huis. | Tomorrow I go home. |
| **Om acht uur** begin ik. | At eight o'clock I start. |
| **In Utrecht** woon ik. | In Utrecht I live. |
