## Introduction

A2 taught **de vergadering**, **solliciteren**, and **het cv**. B1 adds **running meetings**, **notulen**, and **full job applications**. Main lesson: [B1 Lesson 17](/notes/b1/17-work-meetings-and-applications).

## Explanation

### Meeting structure

| Part | Dutch | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Open | **Laten** **we** **beginnen**. | start |
| Agenda | **Het** **eerste** **agendapunt** **is** … | topic |
| Return | **Terugkomen** **op** **punt** **twee** … | revisit |
| Decision | **Wij** **nemen** **een** **besluit**. | decide |
| Actions | **Het** **actiepunt** **voor** **Mila** **is** … | follow up |
| Close | **Laten** **we** **de** **vergadering** **afronden**. | end |

### Common meeting verbs

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **voorzitten** | to chair |
| **notuleren** | to take minutes |
| **agenderen** | to put on the agenda |
| **bijwonen** | to attend |
| **terugkomen** **op** | to return to (a topic) |
| **opvolgen** | to follow up |

### Job application chunks

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Ik** **solliciteer** **op** **de** **vacature** **als** … | I am applying for the vacancy as … |
| **Bijgevoegd** **vindt** **u** **mijn** **cv** **en** **motivatiebrief**. | Attached are my CV and cover letter. |
| **Graag** **nodig** ik **u** **uit** **voor** **een** **sollicitatiegesprek**. | I would like to invite you to an interview. |
| **Waarom** **wilt** **u** **bij** **ons** **werken**? | Why do you want to work with us? |

### u in meetings and interviews

With **HR**, **clients**, or **unknown** interviewers, use **u** and **Zou u … kunnen?**. With close colleagues after the meeting: **je** is fine.

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

❌ **Ik** **solliciteer** **voor** **de** **baan** **als** **de** **accountant**.

✅ **Ik** **solliciteer** **op** **de** **vacature** **als** **accountant**.

Use **op** + **vacature**, and **werken als** / **solliciteren als** without **de**.

❌ **De** **vergadering** **is** **afgelopen** **en** **ik** **schrijf** **notulen** **zonder** **actiepunten**.

✅ End with **actiepunten** and **opvolging**: who does what by when.

## Quick Reference

| Context | Chunk |
| --- | --- |
| Start meeting | Laten we beginnen met punt één |
