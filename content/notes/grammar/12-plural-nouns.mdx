## Explanation

Main patterns: **+en**, **+s**, vowel change, irregular.

| Singular | Plural | Pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| de stoel | de stoelen | +en |
| de tafel | de tafels | +s |
| de man | de mannen | double consonant |
| het kind | de kinderen | irregular |
| de stad | de steden | vowel change |

Plural always uses **de**, never **het**.

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **De appels** zijn goedkoop. | The apples are cheap. |
| **Hoeveel kosten** de schoenen? | How much do the shoes cost? |
