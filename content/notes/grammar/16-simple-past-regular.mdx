## Explanation

Past of regular verbs: stem + **te** or **de** + plural **-n**.

| Rule | Ending | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| stem ends in t, k, f, s, ch, p | -te | werkte, maakte |
| otherwise | -de | woonde, speelde |

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| Ik **werkte** gisteren thuis. | I worked at home yesterday. |
| **Woonde** je in Amsterdam? | Did you live in Amsterdam? |
| Wij **maakten** soep. | We made soup. |
| Hij **woonde** in Leiden. | He lived in Leiden. |
