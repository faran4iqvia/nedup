## Introduction

B1 reading and debate move to **public topics**: housing, elections, climate, and welfare. Main lesson: [B1 Lesson 19](/notes/b1/19-society-and-environment).

## Explanation

### Society and institutions

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **de** **samenleving** | society |
| **de** **maatschappij** | society / community |
| **de** **burger** | citizen |
| **de** **overheid** | government / authorities |
| **de** **regering** | (the) government / cabinet |
| **de** **gemeente** | municipality |

### Politics and policy

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **de** **verkiezing** | election |
| **stemmen** | to vote |
| **het** **beleid** | policy |
| **de** **wet** | law |
| **het** **parlement** | parliament |
| **de** **coalitie** / **oppositie** | coalition / opposition |

### Environment

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **het** **milieu** | environment |
| **duurzaam** | sustainable |
| **het** **klimaat** | climate |
| **de** **klimaatverandering** | climate change |
| **de** **vervuiling** | pollution |
| **de** **hernieuwbare** **energie** | renewable energy |
| **de** **energietransitie** | energy transition |

### Passive in news (review)

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Er** **worden** **maatregelen** **genomen**. | Measures are being taken. |
| **De** **wet** **wordt** **vandaag** **besproken**. | The law is discussed today. |
| **Het** **klimaatakkoord** **is** **ondertekend**. | The climate agreement has been signed. |

From [B1 Lesson 8](/notes/b1/08-passive-with-worden) and [Lesson 9](/notes/b1/09-passive-with-zijn).

### Opinion chunks on public topics

From [B1 Lesson 13](/notes/b1/13-nuanced-opinions) and [Lesson 14](/notes/b1/14-agreeing-and-debating):

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **moet** **de** **overheid** … | In my opinion the government should … |
| **Daar** **ben** ik **het** **deels** **mee** **eens**. | I partly agree with that. |
| **Aan** **de** **ene** **kant** … **aan** **de** **andere** **kant** … | On one hand … on the other hand … |

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">gemeente as "government" generally</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **de gemeente** = municipality (local). National government → **de regering** / **het kabinet** · do not use **gemeente** for all bureaucracy.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">zorgstelsel vs zorgverzekering</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **het zorgstelsel** = healthcare system · **de zorgverzekering** = your health insurance policy. Different words for different admin tasks.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">duurzaam vs duurzaamheid</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    Adjective: **duurzaam** (**duurzame energie**) · Noun: **duurzaamheid** (sustainability). Match word class in **het milieu** discussions.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

<ShadCnCard className="my-4">
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle className="text-lg">bijstand literal "assistance"</CardTitle>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    **de bijstand** = social welfare benefit (specific NL term) · not generic "help". Context: **uitkering**, **gemeente**, **DUO**-style vocabulary.
  </CardContent>
</ShadCnCard>

## Answer Key

Open practice with news. Samples:

1. Highlight words like **klimaat**, **duurzaam**, **woningmarkt**, **gemeente**, **energietransitie**.
2. **Naar mijn mening moet de overheid meer investeren in duurzame energie.**
3. **Aan de ene kant helpt de energietransitie het klimaat, aan de andere kant zijn de kosten hoog.**
4. Feit: **De regering kondigde nieuwe maatregelen aan.** · Mening: **Ik vind dat de maatregelen te laat komen.**
