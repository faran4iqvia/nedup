## Introduction

B1 reading and debate move to **public topics**: housing, elections, climate, and welfare. Main lesson: [B1 Lesson 19](/notes/b1/19-society-and-environment).

## Explanation

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **de** **samenleving** | society |
| **de** **maatschappij** | society / community |
| **de** **burger** | citizen |
| **de** **overheid** | government / authorities |
| **de** **regering** | (the) government / cabinet |
| **de** **gemeente** | municipality |

### Passive in news (review)

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Er** **worden** **maatregelen** **genomen**. | Measures are being taken. |
| **De** **wet** **wordt** **vandaag** **besproken**. | The law is discussed today. |
| **Het** **klimaatakkoord** **is** **ondertekend**. | The climate agreement has been signed. |

### Opinion chunks on public topics

From [B1 Lesson 13](/notes/b1/13-nuanced-opinions) and [Lesson 14](/notes/b1/14-agreeing-and-debating):

| Dutch | English |
| --- | --- |
| **Naar** **mijn** **mening** **moet** **de** **overheid** … | In my opinion the government should … |

| **Aan** **de** **ene** **kant** … **aan** **de** **andere** **kant** … | On one hand … on the other hand … |

## Common Mistakes for English Speakers

❌ **De** **politiek** **is** **duurzaam**. (policy vs politics)

✅ **Het** **beleid** **is** **duurzaam**. / **De** **politiek** **is** **ingewikkeld**.

**Politiek** = politics. **Beleid** = policy.

❌ **Ik** **stem** **voor** **de** **Partij** **X**. (Dutch usage)

✅ **Ik** **stem** **op** **de** **Partij** **X**. / **Ik** **stem** **voor** **kandidaat** **Y**.
