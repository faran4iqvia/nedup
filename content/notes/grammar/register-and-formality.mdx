## Introduction

**Register** is how formal or informal your Dutch sounds. It is not a separate grammar system, but it changes **pronouns**, **openings**, **particles**, and **chunk choice**. Core grammar rules live in [files 01–42](/notes/grammar/00-introduction#core-grammar). Files **43–58** are **phrase and register** resources for real situations.

Use this page when you are unsure whether to say **jij** or **u**, **Hoi** or **Geachte**, or when a B1 lesson points you to formal writing or debate chunks.

## Quick Review

**jij/je** vs **u**, **jouw** vs **uw**, and **Zou u …?** for polite requests — the register spine for Dutch.

## Explanation

### Three tone levels (simplified)

| Level | Dutch label | You use it when … | Pronoun | Example opener |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Informal | **informeel** | friends, close colleagues, peers your age | **jij/je**, **jullie** | **Hoi**, **Hey** |
| Neutral | **neutraal** | colleagues you know, service staff (default) | often **u** first, then match them | **Goedemorgen**, **Dag** |
| Formal | **formeel** | government, unknown officials, job applications, complaints | **u**, **uw** | **Geachte mevrouw**, **Geachte heer** |

**Rule of thumb:** start slightly more formal than you think; let the other person offer **jij** (**We kunnen tutoyeren** / **Noem me maar …**).

### jij vs u

| Situation | Use | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Friend, family, child | **jij** | **Hoe gaat het met je?** |
| Shop, café (young staff) | often **u** first | **Mag ik …?** / **Kunt u …?** |
| Doctor (first visit) | often **u** | **Ik heb pijn, dokter.** |
| Employer, IND, gemeente | **u** | **Kunt u mij helpen?** |
| Email to unknown recipient | **u** | **Zou u … kunnen …?** |

| Wrong register | Better |
| --- | --- |
| ❌ **Hoi, ik wil inschrijven** (gemeente) | ✅ **Goedemorgen. Ik wil me inschrijven.** |
| ❌ **Geachte** to your roommate | ✅ **Hoi** / **Hey** |

See [Grammar 01](/notes/grammar/01-personal-pronouns) and [10](/notes/grammar/10-possessive-pronouns).

### Verb forms with u

**u** takes the same verb form as **jij** in statements, but questions keep full **t**:

| Person | Statement | Question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **jij** | **Je bent** klaar. | **Ben je** klaar? (t-drop) |
| **u** | **U bent** klaar. | **Bent u** klaar? (keep **t**) |

### Politeness with zou and graag

| Chunk | Register | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Zou u … kunnen …?** | formal request | **Zou u dat kunnen uitleggen?** |
| **Ik zou graag …** | polite wish | **Ik zou graag een afspraak maken.** |
| **Mag ik …?** | permission (informal OK) | **Mag ik hier zitten?** |
| **Kunt u …?** | direct formal | **Kunt u mij bellen?** |

Full patterns: [Grammar 35: Zou and Politeness](/notes/grammar/35-zou-politeness-and-advice).

### Written openings and closings

| Register | Opening | Closing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Formal letter/email | **Geachte mevrouw Jansen,** / **Geachte heer De Vries,** | **Met vriendelijke groet,** |
| Semi-formal | **Beste Jan,** / **Beste collega's,** | **Met vriendelijke groet,** / **Groeten,** |
| Informal | **Hoi Jan,** / **Hey,** | **Groetjes,** / **Tot later,** |

Full templates: [46: Formal Letters and Emails](/notes/grammar/46-formal-letters-and-emails).

### Spoken particles and register

Particles (**hoor**, **toch**, **even**, **maar**) are **informal spoken** Dutch. Fine with friends; avoid in **Geachte** emails.

| Particle | Feel | Register |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **hoor** | friendly | informal |
| **toch** | emphasis / "right?" | informal–neutral |
| **even** | "just a sec" | informal–neutral |

Full list: [54: Particles Masterclass](/notes/grammar/54-particles-masterclass).
