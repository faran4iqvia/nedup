## Introduction

This lesson belongs to the B1 stage of the Pronunciation Track. You will study it alongside [B1 Lesson 22](/notes/phase-3-a2-b1/b1/22-media-and-news) when Phase 3 is available.

## What you will learn

- Randstad Dutch vs more formal NOS news Dutch
- Belgian Dutch (Flemish) differences you may hear in **Gent**, **Antwerpen**, or **Brussel**
- Why **g** sounds softer in some regions and harder in others
- Vocabulary pronunciation differences (e.g. **zak** vs **tas** for bag in Belgium)

## Practice

Compare the same news headline read by a Dutch and a Belgian presenter. Note two sound or vocabulary differences.
