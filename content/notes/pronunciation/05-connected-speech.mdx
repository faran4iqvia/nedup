## Introduction

This lesson belongs to the A2 stage of the Pronunciation Track. You will study it alongside [A2 Lesson 10](/notes/phase-2-a1-a2/a2/10-prepositions-place) when Phase 2 is available.

## What you will learn

- How unstressed words (**de**, **het**, **een**, **je**) shorten in fast speech
- Linking between words: **is het** → sounds like one unit
- When final consonants soften or disappear before another consonant
- Common reductions in everyday Randstad Dutch

## Practice

Listen to short Dutch clips and note which words disappear or merge. Repeat sentence chains from your A1 lessons at natural speed before starting A2.
