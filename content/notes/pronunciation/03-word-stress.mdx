## Introduction

This Pronunciation Track lesson supports the main course. Study alongside **A0 Lesson 8 onward**. Say every example word aloud at least twice.

## Explanation

Dutch stress rules (simplified):

1. **Most words stress the first syllable:** **WOON-kamer**, **NEDE-land**, **SPRE-ken**.
2. **Prefixes be-, ge-, ver-, ont- are usually unstressed:** ver**STAAN**, ge**MAKEN**.
3. **Compound words stress the first part:** **fiets**band, **koffie**zetapparaat.

Say new vocabulary with stress marked on the first strong syllable.
