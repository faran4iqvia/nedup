## Introduction

This Pronunciation Track lesson supports the main course. Study alongside **A1 Lesson 1 onward**. Say every example word aloud at least twice.

## Explanation

Dutch intonation patterns:

| Pattern | Example | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Flat statements | Ik werk vandaag. | neutral |
| Verb stress in inversion | **Vandaag** werk ik. | slight pause after fronted element |
| Yes/no questions | Werk je vandaag? | rise at end |
| Wh-questions | Waar woon je? | fall after question word |

Read short sentence chains without pausing: **Ik sta op. Ik douche. Ik ontbijt.**
