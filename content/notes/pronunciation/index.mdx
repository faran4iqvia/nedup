## Welcome to the Pronunciation Track 📣

Dutch sounds are the foundation of everything else — if your vowels and the
famous *g* are right, people understand you even when your grammar wobbles.
This track works at any level and is worth revisiting as you progress.

**What you'll learn:**

- The Dutch vowel system, including long/short pairs and diphthongs like *ui* and *eu*
- Consonants that trip up English speakers: *g*, *ch*, *r*, and *w*
- Word stress and how it changes meaning
- Sentence intonation and rhythm
- Connected speech — how Dutch really sounds at full speed
- Regional variation between the Netherlands and Flanders

Start with [Vowel Sounds](/notes/pronunciation/01-vowel-sounds).
