## Introduction

You have worked through all six Pronunciation Track lessons. This chapter mixes **vowels**, **consonants**, **stress**, **intonation**, **connected speech**, and **regional awareness** in one practice session. Use it after **A0** (Parts A and B only), after **A2** (through Part D), or after **B1** for a full pass. Revisit anytime your speaking feels flat or your listening lags behind your grammar.

Say every drill aloud. Record yourself on items 15 to 20 and compare with a native source if you can.

## Answer Key

Parts A to E are **pronunciation drills**: if you said them aloud with correct stress, linking, and falls/rises, you pass. Use native audio to fine tune.

Part F is open practice. Keep a **taalroutine**: five minutes of pronunciation daily beats one long session monthly.

## Where You Stand

Clear vowels, a workable **g**, correct **stress**, and basic **linking** matter more than a perfect accent. Pair this track with [B1 Lesson 26: Fast Speech Strategies](/notes/b1/26-fast-speech-strategies) and revisit [Connected Speech](/notes/pronunciation/05-connected-speech) whenever listening feels hard. **Veel succes met oefenen!**
