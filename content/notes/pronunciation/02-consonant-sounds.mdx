## Introduction

This Pronunciation Track lesson supports the main course. Study alongside **A0 Lessons 1–7**. Say every example word aloud at least twice.

## Explanation

| Sound | How to make it | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dutch **g** / **ch** | voiceless, back of throat | **goed**, **acht**, **achtig** |
| Dutch **r** | tongue tip or uvular | **raam**, **brood**, **regen** |
| **v** | between teeth, voiced | **vijf**, **vriend** |
| **w** | between v and w | **water**, **woon** |

Practise minimal pairs: **goed** vs English "good", **acht** vs "act".
