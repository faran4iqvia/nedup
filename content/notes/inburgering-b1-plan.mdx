## A2 vs B1: which plan is yours?

Under the **Wet inburgering 2021**, many newcomers follow the **B1 route** (Programma I). Your personal plan (**PIP**) in [Mijn Inburgering](https://www.inburgeren.nl) states whether you need **A2** or **B1** language exams.

| Plan | Language level | Exam name | Dutchyy plan |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **A2 route** | CEFR A2 | Inburgeringstaal A2 | [A2 Inburgering Study Plan](/notes/inburgering-a2-plan) |
| **B1 route** | CEFR B1 | Staatsexamen NT2 Programma I | **This page** |

Both routes include **KNM** (Knowledge of Dutch Society). Check Mijn Inburgering for **ONA** or other parts on your personal plan.

## The exams

### B1 language exams (Staatsexamen NT2 Programma I)

All **four language parts** are on the **computer** at B1, including Schrijven (you **type**, not write on paper). Official details: [staatsexamennt2.nl](https://www.staatsexamennt2.nl) · [Inburgeren.nl](https://www.inburgeren.nl).

| Exam | What it tests | Format | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Lezen** | Reading: work, study, daily life texts | Paper booklet + answers on computer (~44 questions) | ~110 min |
| **Luisteren** | Listening: work, school, daily life | Computer, ~40 multiple choice; each clip **once** | ~90 min |
| **Schrijven** | Writing sentences, forms, short texts | **Computer keyboard** (Dutch layout, no spell check) | ~100 min |
| **Spreken** | Short and medium spoken responses | Computer, microphone; 20 or 30 sec per answer | ~30 min |

Pass mark for each language part: **scale score 500** (see official sites for current rules).

### KNM (same for A2 and B1 routes)

| Exam | What it tests | Format | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **KNM** | Knowledge of Dutch society | Computer, 40 multiple choice questions | ~45 min |

You must pass **every part** on your personal plan.

<Callout title="Dutchyy covers the full path to B1 Dutch" type="info">
  Follow **A0, A1, A2, then B1** on the main path (89 lessons + four reviews). That is the **language** foundation for Programma I. For **KNM**, use Dutchyy for context, then drill official practice exams on [Inburgeren.nl](https://www.inburgeren.nl).
</Callout>

<Callout title="Study habits that matter at B1" type="info">
  1. **Read longer texts without stopping.** B1 Lezen uses folders and articles, not one line ads.
  2. **Type Dutch, do not only handwrite.** B1 Schrijven is on a keyboard.
  3. **Practice timed speaking.** Spreken allows 20 to 30 seconds per answer.
  4. **Listen once, then answer.** Luisteren clips play a single time.
  5. **Pass each level review** before advancing. B1 assumes solid A2 grammar.
</Callout>

## Study timeline

Complete the main course **in order**. B1 builds on A2 grammar: do not skip to B1 without A2 unless you pass the [A2 Review](/notes/a2/review-and-test) comfortably.

**Rough timeline:** ~**30 to 36 weeks** at 25 to 30 min per day (full A0 through B1 + exam prep). ~**22 to 26 weeks** at 45 min per day.

## Weekly rhythm

| Day | 30 to 40 minutes (B1 path needs slightly more) |
| --- | --- |
| **Mon to Thu** | One main lesson: read, exercises, flashcards, dialogue aloud |
| **Fri** | Finish lesson OR rehearse one B1 scenario from memory |
| **Sat** | Pronunciation or listening (Jeugdjournaal / NPO clip) + Word Hub review |
| **Sun** | Rest or catch up; optional news article from [B1 L15](/notes/b1/15-reading-the-news) sources |

Spend **one extra week** on each level review before advancing.

## Exam sprint

Last **4 weeks** before booking:

1. Pass [B1 Review and Test](/notes/b1/review-and-test) with most answers **said aloud**.
2. Run [Grammar Review and Practice](/notes/grammar/review-and-practice) **full pass** (Parts A to D).
3. Run [Pronunciation Review and Practice](/notes/pronunciation/review-and-practice) **full pass**.
4. One official **Programma I oefenexamen** per language part on [staatsexamennt2.nl](https://www.staatsexamennt2.nl).
5. Rehearse **all B1 gemeente scenarios** from memory.
6. **Schrijven:** type two full practice exams on a keyboard (100 min each).
7. **Spreken:** practise 20 and 30 second timed answers daily.
