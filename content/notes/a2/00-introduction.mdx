## Welcome to A2 📈

Time to sound like yourself in Dutch. At this level you move beyond survival
phrases: you tell stories in the past, give opinions, describe people and
places, and start building longer, connected sentences.

**What you'll learn:**

- The perfect tense and past tenses in depth, including irregular verbs
- Giving opinions, comparing things, and describing people and places
- Prepositions of place and time
- Subordinate clauses with _want_ / _omdat_, relative clauses, and _om te_
- _Er_ constructions, conditionals with _als_, and an intro to the passive

Finish with the [A2 Review and Test](/notes/a2/review-and-test) to check you
are ready for B1.

Start with [Lesson 1: Past Tense in Depth](/notes/a2/01-past-tense-depth).
