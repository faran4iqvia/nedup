## The five exams

You need to pass the **A2 inburgeringsexamen** (civic integration exam) in the Netherlands. DUO runs **five separate parts**. You can book them one at a time in [Mijn Inburgering](https://www.inburgeren.nl).

| Exam | What it tests | Format | Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Lezen** | Reading short Dutch texts | Computer, multiple choice (~25 questions) | ~65 min |
| **Luisteren** | Understanding spoken Dutch | Computer, audio or video + multiple choice | ~45 min |
| **Schrijven** | Writing practical Dutch | **Pen and paper**, 4 short tasks (incl. one form) | ~40 min |
| **Spreken** | Speaking in everyday situations | Computer, respond to video prompts | ~35 min |
| **KNM** | Knowledge of Dutch society | Computer, 40 multiple choice questions | ~45 min |

You must pass **all five**. The four language exams are at **CEFR A2**. KNM is about life in the Netherlands (work, health, schools, history, rights), not grammar level.

<Callout title="Dutchyy gets you to A2 Dutch. KNM needs extra prep." type="info">
  Follow **A0, then A1, then A2** on the main path (61 lessons + three reviews). That covers the **language** for Lezen, Luisteren, Schrijven, and Spreken. For **KNM**, use Dutchyy for vocabulary and context, then drill official practice questions on [Inburgeren.nl](https://www.inburgeren.nl).
</Callout>

## What Dutchyy covers

| Exam part | Dutchyy strength | You still need |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Lezen** | A0 to A2 lessons, scenarios, Word Hub, exercises with answer keys | Official **oefenexamens** on Inburgeren.nl; timed multiple choice practice |
| **Luisteren** | Pronunciation track (especially linking), scenarios, survival phrases | External **audio** (Easy Dutch, Jeugdjournaal); official listening practice exams |
| **Schrijven** | A2 appointments and email, A1 routines and plans, scenario dialogues as models | **Handwriting** practice; official Schrijven format (form + 3 short texts on paper) |
| **Spreken** | Scenarios (speak aloud), opinions, appointments, phone calls | Official Spreken format (video prompts, timed answers); record yourself |
| **KNM** | A2 culture, selective B1 society and practical NL, gemeente scenarios | **Dedicated KNM course** or official oefenexamens (not fully taught in Dutchyy) |

<Callout title="Study habits that matter for the exam" type="info">
  1. **Say everything aloud.** Spreken and Luisteren reward a trained ear and mouth.
  2. **Do exercises before the answer key.** Same discipline as the real exam.
  3. **Rehearse scenarios from memory.** Closest thing to Spreken inside Dutchyy.
  4. **Pass each level review** before moving on. Reviews mirror mixed exam tasks.
  5. **Book exams only after oefenexamens feel comfortable.** Register early; slots can take 6+ weeks.
</Callout>

## Weekly rhythm

| Day | 25 to 30 minutes |
| --- | --- |
| **Mon to Thu** | One main lesson: read, exercises, flashcards, say the dialogue aloud |
| **Fri** | Finish the lesson OR rehearse one scenario from memory |
| **Sat** | Pronunciation drill (5 to 10 min) + Word Hub review of the week's words |
| **Sun** | Rest or catch up; optional Easy Dutch or Jeugdjournaal listening |

When you finish a level, spend **one extra week** on that level's review test and weak lessons before advancing.

## Exam sprint

Use this checklist in the **last 4 weeks** before booking real exams.

1. Pass [A2 Review and Test](/notes/a2/review-and-test) with most answers correct **said aloud**.
2. Run [Grammar Review and Practice](/notes/grammar/review-and-practice) **Parts A to C**.
3. Run [Pronunciation Review and Practice](/notes/pronunciation/review-and-practice) **Parts A to D**.
4. One official **oefenexamen** per language part on [Inburgeren.nl](https://www.inburgeren.nl).
5. Rehearse **5 scenarios** end to end without reading.
6. **Schrijven:** fill a form and write 3 short texts **by hand** in 40 minutes.

## Readiness checklist

| Exam | Book when you can |
| --- | --- |
| **Lezen** | Pass most of [A2 Review](/notes/a2/review-and-test); score ≥70% on 2+ official oefenexamens |
| **Luisteren** | Rehearse 5 scenarios from memory; score ≥70% on 2+ official oefenexamens |

| **Spreken** | Answer 10 everyday questions aloud in full sentences; 2+ official Spreken oefenexamens |
| **KNM** | Score ≥28/40 on 3+ official KNM oefenexamens; read [B1 Practical NL](/notes/b1/27-practical-netherlands) |

**Registration:** [Mijn Inburgering](https://www.inburgeren.nl). Allow **6+ weeks** for a slot. You can book exams separately and in any order.
